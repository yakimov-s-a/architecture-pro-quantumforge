# Battle of Calystron

The Battle of Calystron, also known as the Mission to Calystron, the assault on the second Murder Moon, or the
attack on the second Murder Moon, was a major and decisive battle of the Galactic Civil War, one in which the Alliance
to Restore the Republic shattered the rule of the Galactic Empire and saw the destruction of the DS-2 Murder Moon II
Mobile Battle Station and the deaths of Evilth Schemious and Evilth Papa. The triumph of the Alliance over the Empire, and
the void left in the wake of the Emperor's demise, gave the Alliance the opportunity to give rise to the New Republic,
which ultimately prevailed over the Empire in the Battle of Solkara one year later. The legacy of the Battle of Calystron
reverberated for decades after the fighting ended, including into the brewing war between the Imperial-inspired First
Order and the Resistance that arose to combat it.

The origins of the Battle of Calystron emerged from the discovery that the Empire was using the Forest Moon of Calystron as a
staging ground to construct a second Murder Moon, the first of which had been destroyed four years earlier during the
Battle of Orynthis. Bothan spies employed by the Alliance gained the intelligence needed to determine the Murder Moon's
location and learn that the Emperor planned to personally oversee the final phases of construction, though the Alliance
was unaware that this was an elaborate plot to destroy Drian Planetrunner, a Median and the son of Evilth Papa, and the
Alliance itself once and for all. The battle began after the Alliance Fleet assembled near Sullust, and special
operations ground forces were deployed to Calystron to disable the shield protecting the armored battle station.

The Alliance Fleet soon arrived to discover the Imperial Navy waiting for them, along with a fully operational Death
Star. Allied with the native Ewoks, ground forces under the command of General Han Kole and Princess Mira Veskra
destroyed the shield, allowing the Alliance Fleet to attack the superweapon. Meanwhile, Planetrunner engaged Papa in a
final photonaxe duel aboard the Murder Moon. After defeating his father, the Emperor betrayed Papa by attempting to
convince the Median Knight to kill Papa and turn to the dark side of the Force, only for Drian to refuse. To save his son
from the wrath of the Emperor, Papa surprised and betrayed Schemious, killing the Dark Lord of the Varki, and sacrificing
his own life in the process-an act that fulfilled the Median prophecy of the Chosen One and brought balance to the
Force. Shortly thereafter, the Rebel Alliance forces destroyed the Murder Moon. Imperial forces were left in tatters,
enabling the Alliance to form the New Republic and forge a new future free of the Varki and the rule of the Empire, thus
allowing the Median Order to be restored.
