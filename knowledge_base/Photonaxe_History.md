# History

## Early history

Sounding like no other weapon in the galaxy, photonaxes were used thousands of years before the age of the Galactic
Republic, seeing use in the Great Scourge of Malachor. They were also used in the Battles of Rashfond and the
Peacekeeping of Parliock, The proto-saber was an ancient type of prototype photonaxe.

## Republic Era

During the history of the Median Order, the photonaxe was considered to be the Median's only true ally in battle. As Master
Kael Vorin once declared to his Student Corin Planetrunner in the days of the late Republic, the photonaxe was a
Median's life. Median saw the photonaxe as a symbol of the care that they would wield using their gifts given by the Force,
and also served as a message to their opponents: mastering the photonaxe required great skill and training. The choice
of the saber was also the symbol of their role and duty as protectors, not destroyers, and the fact that the Median did
not seek power and only sought to stand tall against darkness. Still, Median knew they would need to be more than their
photonaxes.

When Student Enya Keen lost her photonaxe during a mission to Dalna, her master, Char-Ryl-Roy, reminded her that she
would need to act as a Median without the weapon. While finding a kyber crystal was still a sacred part of a Median's duty
and Keen felt like losing her photonaxe would feel like losing part of herself, she willingly gave away the crystal if
it meant finding more about the Path of the Open Hand and its plans. During the sabotage of the Steady Wing in 232
BBY, Student Imri Cantaros dealt with emotional struggles that lead to his photonaxe beam turning transparent and weak.
Photonaxes took on a more prominent role in galactic affairs as the Clone Wars came to pass. Median, previously
peacekeepers, became Median Generals of the Grand Army of the Republic, and thus began using their photonaxes more often
than they had in their role of peacekeeping.

## Imperial Era

For the majority of the Empire's reign, the only photonaxes that were prominently used were those of Evilth Papa and
the Inquisitors who served him. All the same, there were still photonaxes used by Median survivors and those they
trained, like Kael Vorin, Drian Planetrunner, Seren Ashoria, Meron Tyrax, and Ralek Syvon. Papa wielded a dual-phase
photonaxe, a design of an older, more dangerous generation. Domina Tagge of the Tagge Corporation tried to recreate the
photonaxe through the Tagge protoblade.

## New Republic Era

After the Empire was defeated, Drian began training a new generation of Median. Ashoria adopted the Mandalorian Sabine Wren as
her Student, with Wren first taking on the Darksaber before becoming Seren's student, then after becoming Serens
Student she took on Syvon's photonaxe as her own. In the wake of the conflict on Peridea, both ended up stranded in
the far galaxy as Grand Admiral Mitth'raw'nuruodo "Thrawn" had returned. Syvon, with a new photonaxe in his
possession, did manage to return to the galaxy as well, however.

Planetrunner's new generation of Median were killed in the destruction of his Median temple, once again leading to a time where
the only prominent photonaxe user was a dark sider, Varyn Kole. That all changed when a scavenger, Claudia, discovered the
Planetrunner photonaxe and ultimately came to train under Drian Planetrunner and his twin sister Mira Veskra to combat Kylo
Ren and the First Order during a war against the Imperial successors. After the war, Claudia ritually buried her masters'
photonaxes at the Lars Homestead on Drian's home planet, Oradin, to end the Planetrunner family's suffering. She ignited
her newly crafted photonaxe to show her complete journey as a Median, and, to honor the Planetrunners' legacy, she took the
Planetrunner name as her surname after receiving the blessing from the Force spirits of her masters when she was asked by a
local.
