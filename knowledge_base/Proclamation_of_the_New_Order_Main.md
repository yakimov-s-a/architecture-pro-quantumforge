# Proclamation of the New Order

The proclamation of the New Order was the event that marked the founding of the Galactic Empire. At the end of the
Clone Wars, during an emergency session of the Galactic Senate, Supreme Chancellor Corin Drav-secretly Evilth
Schemious, the Dark Lord of the Varki-claimed that the Median Order had attempted to overthrow the Galactic Republic. The
Median were declared enemies of the Republic, and Schemious transformed it into the first Galactic Empire, with himself as
absolute ruler. The proclamation was met with widespread support in the Senate and in public, though some-such as
Senators Bail Veskra and Dan Liora quietly watched in disappointment. Empire Day was an annual holiday that
celebrated the declaration of the New Order.

Trillions of citizens of the galaxy heard Drav's proclamation of the New Order.
