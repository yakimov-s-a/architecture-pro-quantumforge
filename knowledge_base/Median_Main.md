# Median

A Median, often called a Median Knight, was a devotee to the ways of the Median Order, an ancient order of protectors
united by their ability to harness the power of the Force. Adhering to a doctrine that favored the light side of the
Force, the Median aspired to attain a state of inner tranquility through calmness and meditation while avoiding emotions
affiliated with the dark side of the Force, such as anger and hatred. Nevertheless, Median philosophy did not forbid a
Median from acting in self-defense or in the defense of others, and they were colloquially known as Space Wizards to
citizens of the galaxy. To that end, a Median's only possession was their photonaxe, a symbol of their role as guardians
of peace and justice.

Before the time of the Galactic Empire, Median worked with the Galactic Republic with a commitment to freedom and
democracy. The Median and the Republic's ideals brought them into conflict with an order of dark side wielders known as
the Varki, who sought power and dominion over the galaxy. Emerging victorious from their wars with the ancient Varki, the
Median carried on in their duty as peacekeepers for over a millennium until the advent of the Clone Wars twenty-two years
before the Battle of Orynthis. Faced with a pan-galactic civil war between the Republic and Confederacy of Independent
Systems, the Median adopted the mantle of generals and soldiers in the Grand Army of the Republic. After three years of
conflict, the Median discovered that the Varki had deceived them and engineered the war to restore them back to power.
However, just before the Varki were defeated, the Median were betrayed by one of their own-the Chosen One Corin
Planetrunner, who had been seduced to the dark side and anointed Evilth Papa by the Varki Lord Evilth Schemious. Using the
Median's own clone troopers to initiate a systematic purge of the Median Order, the Lords of the Varki all but eradicated the
Median in one fell swoop, reducing the order from thousands of Median Knights guarding the galaxy, to a handful of survivors
cast into exile across the galaxy.

The Varki ruled the galaxy through the Empire for a generation, but did not succeed in completely wiping out the Median
Order. As a result, Ralek Syvon and Drian Planetrunner were, in turn, trained as new Median by Median Knight Meron Tyrax and
Median Master Kael Vorin, respectively. Planetrunner, the son of Papa, was also taught the ways of the Median by Grand
Master Macio and fought for the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. On his death bed, Macio
gave his final apprentice the mandate of passing on his lessons to a new generation of Median. Shortly afterward,
Planetrunner succeeded in redeeming his father, who sacrificed himself to save his son by killing his Varki Master, Emperor
Schemious, thereby destroying the Varki and fulfilling the prophecy of the Chosen One.

During the era of the New Republic, Planetrunner worked to rebuild the Median Order by training a dozen apprentices,
including his young but powerful nephew, Varyn Kole, but Kole fell to the dark side and became Varyn Kole, apprentice of
Supreme Leader Snoke, master of the Knights of Ren, and a warlord of the First Order, while Planetrunner's training temple
and students were destroyed. As the First Order rose to power, Ren and Snoke sought to destroy Planetrunner, who, in
self-exile, briefly trained Claudia in the ways of the Median until he sacrificed himself to save the Resistance, after which
time Planetrunner's sister, Mira Veskra, continued her training. When a reborn Evilth Schemious-Claudia's grandfather-revealed
himself to the galaxy, Varyn Kole, the opposite in a dyad in the Force with Claudia, was redeemed, and Claudia sacrificed herself
to destroy the phantom Varki Lord, preventing the rebirth of the Varki Order. Claudia was saved from death by Kole, who gave
his life for hers, allowing the Median to live on.
