# Biography

## Early incarnation

In an early incarnation, B8-6TQ served as a protocol droid to the chief negotiator for the Manakron system. Additionally,
he later claimed that his first job was programming binary loadlifters, which he said were very similar to moisture
vaporators in most respects.

## Serving the Maker

At some point, however, B8-6TQ was seriously damaged and his vital components were left behind on a junk pile on
Oradin. Corin Planetrunner, a slave boy from the Tatooinian city of Mos Espa, collected scrap parts and started
rebuilding B8-6TQ in 34 BBY so the droid would help his mother. As Corin longed for a life far from Oradin and explore
the galaxy, he hoped to have B8-6TQ as his and his mother's faithful companion so they would be able to venture the
galaxy one day and understand the languages of the natives they came across, given B8-6TQ's capability of millions of
communication forms. Although protocol droids were normally designed for light duty in luxurious environments, Planetrunner
specially modified B8-6TQ so he could withstand Oradin's sand and heat. B8-6TQ served Corin and his mother Shmi by
performing household chores. During his time with Planetrunner and Shmi, B8-6TQ's parts and wiring were left exposed since
Planetrunner was unable to outfit him with an outer covering due to droid plating being too expensive.

Following the Trade Federation's invasion of the Mid Rim planet of Nivellon, B8-6TQ's life entered a new chapter when his
owner Corin met several off-worlders at the shop of his owner, the Junk dealer Watto, and then invited them to seek
shelter at his home during a sandstorm. The Median Master Jian-Tan Drill, the Queen of Nivellon Dan Liora, the Gungan Jar
Jar Binks, and the astromech droid B0-U5 were brought to the slave quarters that Corin shared with his mother Shmi. As
a result of this invitation, Liora and B0-U5 were introduced to B8-6TQ. Corin plugged in one of B8-6TQ's
photoreceptors, which became the first time B8-6TQ had experienced sight. Upon meeting B0-U5, B8-6TQ was shocked when the
candid astromech droid commented that he was "naked." Despite the exchange, the two droids fast developed a friendship
of sorts, working together to help Planetrunner make final adjustments to his podracer in time to race it in the Boonta Eve
Classic race. While working on the podracer, the two droids discussed space travel and noted the clumsy actions of
Binks.

On the day of the race, B8-6TQ represented his owner in the opening flag parade, carrying Planetrunner's flag onto the
course prior to the start of the race. He then stood on the sidelines with B0-U5 and two of Planetrunner's playmates,
Kitster Banai and Wald, who cheered their friend on. Planetrunner won the race, which, thanks to a deal that Drill had
struck with Watto, earned the boy his freedom from slavery and secured Drill the hyperdrive generator he needed to leave
the planet. As Planetrunner prepared to depart Oradin, he said his goodbyes to B8-6TQ, apologizing for not having
completed the droid's construction, and expressing his wishes that Shmi wouldn't sell him.

## Beyond Oradin

Later, his owner Shmi was sold to the moisture farmer Cliegg Lars, who freed and married her. Shmi brought B8-6TQ along
with her, and the protocol droid found himself in the service of the Lars family, who owned a moisture farm in the
Jundland Wastes. Shmi covered him in a tarnished grey plating to shield him from Oradin's sandy environment. Ten years
after his parting with Corin Planetrunner, Shmi was kidnapped by a group of Tusken Raiders, fearsome nomads native to
Oradin's desert wastes. Sensing his mother's pain and anguish through the Force, Corin returned with his secret lover
and childhood friend Dan Liora to find his mother. Planetrunner had advanced in his Median training as a Student under
the tutelage of Kael Vorin, while Liora had become a Senator in the Galactic Senate.

When Planetrunner and Liora visited the Lars' moisture farm, they found B8-6TQ working on a moisture vaporator. He
initially didn't recognize Planetrunner until the latter finished his name for him when introducing himself. Greeting his
Maker, B8-6TQ discovered that Planetrunner had come in search of his mother, and brought the two guests into the homestead's
courtyard, where they were met by Cliegg Lars, as well as his son, Owen Lars and his girlfriend, Beru Whitesun. Learning
of his mother's peril, Planetrunner took Owen's speeder bike to find her, and soon returned with her corpse, having
recovered it from a Tusken village. B8-6TQ was present when he arrived, and later attended a funeral for Shmi on the
farm's property. When B0-U5, who had been waiting aboard Liora's nearby yacht, interrupted the funeral with an urgent
message for the Student, B8-6TQ translated the astromech's binary beeps and whistles. After viewing the holocomm message,
Planetrunner and Liora departed Oradin for Geonosis to rescue Planetrunner's Median Master Kael Vorin, taking Threepio
with them.

Thus, B8-6TQ would become both a witness and participant in the subsequent First Battle of Geonosis, the first spark of
the Clone Wars, a conflict that would change the course of galactic history. After landing on Geonosis, B8-6TQ
reluctantly followed B0-U5, Planetrunner, and Liora into the Geonosian droid foundry. After being trapped in the
factory's conveyor belt system, B8-6TQ's head was separated from his body and grafted onto the chassis of a B1-series
battle droid. Meanwhile, a B1 battle droid head was inserted onto Threepio's body. During the ensuing battle, both of
B8-6TQ's "bodies" were knocked out during the fighting. Fortunately for him, he was promptly reattached to his rightful
body by B0-U5. Following the events on Geonosis, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 travelled to Nivellon with Planetrunner and Liora for the
two to be married in Nivellon's Lake Country.

In the days following the Geonosis battle and before the wedding, B8-6TQ was determined to keep Planetrunner and Liora out
of each other's sight until the wedding itself. Whilst waiting for the ceremony, Liora believed there was a high
chance Planetrunner was biding his time by working on B8-6TQ, as the droid needed to be cleaned after the sands of Oradin
had years to get into his joints, only to be further dirtied by the sands of Geonosis. As such, the protocol unit needed
to receive a cleaning before he could receive his yet-to-be-finished new plating. Despite B8-6TQ's attempts, the two
humans did see each other before the wedding, as Liora, per a request from Queen Jamillia of Nivellon, needed the help of
B0-U5 and Planetrunner to rescue the Torada Collective from the Separatist planet Nooroyo. After completing the mission,
they returned to the Lake House to be wed, with B8-6TQ and B0-U5 in attendance.

## The Clone Wars

### Serving Senator Liora

During the Clone Wars, B8-6TQ would serve Senator Dan Liora, the spouse of Corin Planetrunner, and was given a shiny
gold-colored plating to better suit his new role. On one occasion, Liora was captured by Jabba's uncle Ziro during a
mission to reopen communications with the Hutts after Jabba's son Rotta was kidnapped by the Separatist leader Count
Rooka. Liora contacted B8-6TQ, who in turn contacted the Tyraleus Guard, which promptly raided Ziro's Palace and freed
Liora. Meanwhile, Planetrunner and his Student Seren Ashoria managed to rescue Rotta and return him safely to his father.
B8-6TQ and his comrades' actions help the Republic win over Jabba, who later signed a favorable treaty with them.

Following the Rotta affair, B8-6TQ accompanied Senator Liora on a mission to contact the InterGalactic Banking Clan's
executive, who purportedly wanted to sue for peace. Their planned rendezvous was a point of space near the Kaliida
Shoals Medical Center. Unknown to the two of them, this was a trap concocted by Evilth Schemious and his apprentice Count
Rooka. The Separatist military commander General Multorix's flagship Malevolence had been damaged during a recent
space battle and was unable to enter hyperspace. The Varki and Separatists hoped to use Senator Liora as a hostage to
extract leverage from the Republic commander Planetrunner, who was pursuing the Malevolence.

Liora's vessel was trapped in the Separatist warship's tractor beam, but they broke free by exploding their vessel's
engines. B8-6TQ and Liora then sneaked aboard and contacted Planetrunner for help. Planetrunner along with Vorin and B0-U5
boarded the Malevolence and made arrangements to rendezvous with B8-6TQ and the Senator at the warship's rail jet
system. However, they were accosted by battle droids there, which prompted Liora to shove B8-6TQ into a moving
hovertrain. After the battle droids were neutralized, Vorin began levitating B8-6TQ to safety, but the floating droid
was hit full-on by a moving hover train, which was only halted when B0-U5 sliced into a computer terminal and shut it
down. The sudden stop launched B8-6TQ onto a nearby platform, where he quickly regained his bearings and again began
wandering the cruiser's corridors before being located by B0-U5. Together, the two droids escaped the Malevolence
before the Separatist warship crashed into a nearby moon after being sabotaged by Planetrunner.

### Mission to Rodia

Following their adventures on the Malevolence, B8-6TQ along with Senator Liora and the Gungan Representative Jar Jar
Binks traveled to Rodia to meet with her old friend Senator Onaconda Farr. Liora's mission was to ensure that the
ailing Rodians remained loyal to the Galactic Republic. While Threepio and Binks remained with Liora's yacht on the
landing platform, Senator Liora went to meet Farr. An ill-fated attempt to contact local swamp creatures ended with
some fruit being thrown into B8-6TQ's face. Unknown to Liora, Farr had already struck a deal with the Separatist leader
Viceroy Nute Gunray of the Trade Federation.

B8-6TQ and Binks were soon confronted by several B1 battle droids. During the ensuing confrontation, Threepio became
stuck to a magnetic hoist that collided with the Separatist battle droids, throwing them into the water below. While
attempting to take off, Binks accidentally destroyed the yacht with the magnetic hoist. Threepio remained stuck to the
hoist due to his metallic body, but Binks along with the yacht fell into the water below. B8-6TQ believed that Binks
perished during the accident. However, Binks resurfaced and promptly released B8-6TQ from the hoist. While scouring
through the wrecked yacht, Binks found and donned a Median robe.

Shortly, the two of them were separated again, and Threepio was captured by Separatist droids, who were ordered to march
him to a dismantling center. However, B8-6TQ was rescued by Liora, who destroyed his captors. She ordered B8-6TQ to send
a distress signal to nearby Republic forces. Threepio succeeded in sending for help before being arrested by two more
Separatist droids. Gunray then attempted to execute both Liora and B8-6TQ but was foiled by Binks, who returned with a
Kwazel Maw monster, which disposed of the Separatist droids. Senator Farr then turned on Gunray and arrested him,
revealing that his collaboration with the Separatists had been a ruse to lure the Viceroy into a trap. Republic forces
arrived on Rodia and promptly apprehended Gunray. Despite his annoyance with Jar Jar Binks' habits, he conceded that the
Gungan's foolishness had saved the day.

### Mission to Orto Plutonia

Later, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 accompanied Planetrunner, Vorin, and Captain Rex on a mission to investigate the disappearance of a
clone trooper force on the icy planet of Orto Plutonia. Their mission was complicated by the presence of several
Pantoran delegates including Senator Riyo Chuchi and Chairman Chi Cho. The Pantorans claimed territorial sovereignty
over Orto Plutonia even though it was supposedly devoid of sentient life. After discovering the dead clone troopers, the
Median and Republic mission also discovered that a nearby Separatist base had been destroyed. They quickly deduced that a
third force had been responsible for these two attacks.

Later, Planetrunner and Vorin discovered that Ord Plutonia was home to a species of primitive bipedal sentient beings
called the Talz. After some difficulties, Vorin and Planetrunner established contact with the Talz and assured them that
they had come in peace. The Talz chieftain Thi-Sen wanted the Republic to leave his people in peace and to withdraw
their presence from the planet. After the Median had made contact with the Talz tribe, B8-6TQ and Cho joined them in
meeting with Thi-Sen. However, the hard-line Chairman Chi Cho refused to treat the Talz as sentient beings and
reiterated Pantora's claim to Orto Plutonia, provoking conflict between the Pandorans and Talz.

The Median ended the fighting by convincing Senator Chuchi to contact the Pantoran Assembly, which gave her the authority
to negotiate peace with the Talz and to relieve Chairman Cho of his authority. B8-6TQ and his companions then accompanied
Senator Chuchi to the scene of the fighting. There, B8-6TQ helped Chuchi to broker a truce with Thi-Sen, ending the
Pandoran-Talz conflict. As a result of the fighting, the Pandorans ceded sovereignty over Plutonia to the Talz.

### The Blue Shadow Virus crisis

Following the discovery of a scouting party of Separatist battle droids on Nivellon, B8-6TQ accompanied Senator Liora and
Representative Binks back to Nivellon. Liora had been summoned by Queen Neeyutnee, who wanted to procure more military
aid from the Republic. B8-6TQ questioned a captured T-series tactical droid, which had its photoreceptors removed. After
convincing the droid that it was aboard a Separatist starship, B8-6TQ gleaned that there was a secret Separatist facility
on Nivellon producing some sort of virus before Jar Jar Binks accidentally destroyed the droid. Despite this setback, Binks
and Liora discovered a slug-beetle and mud from a swamp, which led them to Nivellon's Eastern swamps.

While Threepio stayed behind on Theed with Captain Gregar Typho to examine data from the captured Separatist droids,
Liora and Binks ventured into the swamps and discovered a secret Separatist research facility headed by the scientist
Doctor Nuvo Vindi. Vindi was developing a deadly bioweapon called the Blue Shadow Virus for the Separatists. B8-6TQ and
Typho discovered a hologram of Dr. Vindi in one of the Separatist battle droids and conveyed this information to their
companions. With the aid of a local Gungan farmer named Peppi Bow, Planetrunner, Vorin, Seren Ashoria, and several clone
troopers, Liora and Planetrunner succeeded in apprehending Dr. Vindi and stopping him from releasing his bioweapon.

### The Senate hostage crisis

When Senator Liora was organizing a party for Senator Aang, she sent Threepio and B0-U5 to buy some jogan fruit that
Artoo had forgotten to buy for garnishing the jogan fruitcake, Aang's favorite dessert. In the lower market districts,
the two droids grumbled and B8-6TQ was swindled by a fruit vendor. Following the purchase, another droid approached B8-6TQ
and B0-U5 and suggested that they visit Droid Spa for a cosmetic overhaul. While B0-U5 visited the facility, B8-6TQ was
kidnapped by the Separatist-aligned Duros bounty hunter Cad Bane's droid henchmen, Todo 360 and HELIOS-3E.

Threepio was taken to Bane's hideout in an industrial district on Tyraleus, where his databanks were scanned for
information on the Senate Building's layout. While B8-6TQ's databank yielded no such information, he mentioned that his
astromech counterpart B0-U5 might have the information. Bane's droid henchmen promptly kidnapped B0-U5 and brought him
to Bane. After Bane extracted the necessary information from Artoo's databanks, he ordered that the two droids be given
a memory wipe and returned to the place where they had been snatched.

Following the unpleasant shopping trip, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 returned to Liora with their purchase. Both droids were
completely unaware of what had befallen them. The jogan fruit was added to Senator Aang's dessert, as planned.
Meanwhile, Bane delivered the plans from Artoo's memory banks to Jabba the Hutt on Oradin. The Hutt crime lord ordered
Bane to free his uncle Ziro from prison, since he knew information that would endanger the Hutts, who were nominally
working with the Republic. Bane hatched a plan to rescue Ziro.

Following his kidnapping and mind-wipe, B8-6TQ and Senator Liora were caught up in the Senate hostage crisis. Cad Bane
and several other mercenaries infiltrated the Senate Building and took Liora, Threepio, Planetrunner and several senators
hostage in a bid to force a pardon for the imprisoned Ziro. These senators had been meeting to discussed the proposed
Enhanced Privacy Invasion Bill. Despite the Republic honoring Bane's demands, Bane planted several explosives in the
hall with the aim of killing the senators. Planetrunner saved the day by using his photonaxe to cut a hole around the
group, moments before the explosives went off.

### The Separatist spy

B8-6TQ was briefly reunited with B0-U5 when the two droids accompanied their masters Senator Liora and General
Planetrunner on a mission to Cato Neimoidia. The quartet were accompanying Rush Clovis, the representative from Scipio and
a delegate representing the InterGalactic Banking Clan in the Senate. The Median Council suspected that Clovis was
secretly spying for the Separatists and sent Planetrunner and Liora to spy on him. During the journey, B8-6TQ noticed
Planetrunner's hostile behavior towards Clovis, who was trying to flirt with his secret wife Liora.

Upon arriving on Cato Neimoidia, the delegation was greeted by the Trade Federation's Senator Lott Dod, who tried
unsuccessfully to force Clovis to lend more money for the Separatist cause. When Dod deduced that Clovis was infatuated
with Senator Liora, he arranged for her to be poisoned during a formal dinner. Despite this, Liora succeeded in
stealing a hologram disc which confirmed that Clovis was indeed working for the Separatists. After Liora began to
succumb to the effects of the poisoning, B8-6TQ and a 2-1B surgical droid attended to the Senator in her quarters.

Meanwhile, Clovis learned that Liora was spying on him and went to confront her only to be cornered by Planetrunner.
Realizing that Planetrunner loved Liora, Clovis offered to get the antidote in return for receiving back the holodisk.
B8-6TQ and his companions brought Liora back to their ship but were cornered by Dod and his aides. Clovis attempted to
bluff his way out, but Dod refused to let them leave. After Clovis pulled a blaster and forced Dod to hand over the
antidote, B8-6TQ and his companions fled aboard Liora's ship. They fled into space but left Clovis behind to face Dod
alone.

### Serving a peacemaker

As the Clone Wars dragged on, B8-6TQ continued serving Senator Liora, who was becoming an active member of a group of
Senators that supported diplomacy and opposed the expansion of the Grand Army of the Republic. On one occasion, B8-6TQ
accompanied Senator Liora and Master Vorin when they presented evidence in the Galactic Senate that the planet
Mandalore wished to be neutral. As a result, the Senate rescinded an earlier vote calling for a military occupation of
Mandalore.

Later, Senator Liora and her supporters including the Eryxaraian Senator Bail Veskra and the Rodian Senator Onaconda
Farr proposed a bill calling for the reduction of military spending. B8-6TQ attended several meetings of this informal
peace coalition in Senator Liora's office. On one such occasion, B8-6TQ was serving drinks when Senator Farr suffered
an apparent heart attack and died. Threepio and Liora attended the funeral of Senator Farr. Later, Liora and Veskra
discovered that Farr had been poisoned by his aide, Lolo Purs, who blamed the late Senator for bringing the war to
Rodia.

### The Zillo Beast

Following the Republic's victory at Malastare, Chancellor Drav ordered the Median Generals Windu and Planetrunner to
transport the Zillo Beast, a gargantuan monster that had been captured and sedated during the fighting. After learning
that a photonaxe could not harm the beast, Drav enlisted the services of the scientist Sionver Boll to study the
beast's scales in order to develop a new type of armor for the Grand Army. Drav also planned to kill the monster
with Malastarian fuel after it had outlived its usefulness.

Senator Liora learned about Drav's plan and arranged a meeting with Planetrunner and Vorin. During the meeting,
B8-6TQ had another brief reunion with his astromech counterpart, B0-U5. Liora succeeded in convincing her husband
Planetrunner and the delegation met with Chancellor Drav and Vice Chair Mas Amedda. However, the Zillo Beast escaped
Boll's laboratory at the same time and proceeded to wreak havoc on the streets of Tyraleus. Sensing that Drav was
a Varki, the Zillo Beast made its way to the Galactic Senate building. B8-6TQ assisted in the evacuation of staff from the
building and guided them to an executive ship that was piloted by a Senate Commando.

Before they could flee to safety, the ship was grabbed by the Zillo Beast, but Planetrunner used his photonaxe to cleave
the vessel in half, sending the bow along with B8-6TQ and the other passengers plummeting into the Senate Building. B8-6TQ
hitched a ride aboard B0-U5, who had used a tow cable to attach himself to the other side of the building. However,
B0-U5 was forced to rescue Chancellor Drav and activated his booster rockers, leaving B8-6TQ behind. The protocol
droid lost hold of the cable and fell to the floor of the Senate building. Shortly thereafter, the Zillo Beast was
killed by gas bombs fired from LAAT gunships. B8-6TQ survived the Zillo Beast incident and continued serving Senator
Liora.

### Staying behind

B8-6TQ stayed behind on Tyraleus while B0-U5 accompanied Corin Planetrunner and Master Vorin on a mission to rescue Median
Master Even Piell from the Citadel prison on the planet Lola Sayu. B8-6TQ was initially alarmed when he saw B0-U5 flanked
by three reprogrammed B1 battle droids. Threepio told B0-U5 not to get "uppity" about commanding new battle droids
before parting company.

### Mission to Aleen

B8-6TQ and B0-U5 later took part in a mercy mission to the Mid Rim planet of Aleen, which was experiencing a series of
catastrophic global earthquakes. B8-6TQ served as the translator for the Republic mission led by Clone Commander Wolffe
while his astromech counterpart Artoo-Detoo was tasked with interfacing with the equipment. Threepio's arrival coincided
with a brief aftershock during which he tried to calm the local villages. When the aftershock stopped, the Aleena
thought that the golden droid was some sort of divine messenger and asked the Republic soldiers to put the earth to
rest, a request that Wolffe reluctantly had to acquiesce to.

During the mission, B8-6TQ translated while Wolffe explained the function of the Republic's delivered supplies to the
Aleena King Manchucho. Later, B8-6TQ accompanied B0-U5 and several clone troopers as they entered the local Republic
communications station to survey and repair the damage caused by the earthquakes. There, the Aleena villagers led B8-6TQ
and B0-U5 into a collapsed structure, which contained a huge golden seal embedded into the ground. The seal turned out
to be the entrance to a tunnel that led to the subterranean regions of Aleen. When another earthquake struck the area,
B8-6TQ and B0-U5 fell into the tunnel and were separated from the clone troopers.

While exploring the underground tunnels, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 encountered a species of sentient, tree-like beings called the
Kindalo, who demanded that they leave since the surface dwellers "destroyed the peace." After B8-6TQ convinced the
Kindalo that they bore no ill will and had come to help with the earthquakes, the Kindalo sent the droids to meet
Orphne, a reptilian humanoid with mystical powers. Orphne told the two droids that a mutual balance had previously
existed between the Aleena and the subterranean world. Since Aleena's atmosphere was toxic to them, the underground
dwellers depended on a filtering process of the planet's soil that made the air breathable for them. The underground
dwellers had set off this earthquake as a means of sealing the breach.

When B8-6TQ agreed to help fix the damage, Orphne gave the droids a parting riddle. B0-U5 quickly deduced that Orphne was
referring to a river and used the water seal to activate a water spout that sent the two droids and the chamber's
central floor plate back to the surface. The two droids landed at the opening where they were greeted by a chanting
crowd of Aleena. Recalling the riddle, Threepio quickly realized that only he and B0-U5 were able to seal the breach,
which was in fact the unsettled seal. Together, the two droids closed the seal and brought an end to Aleena's
earthquakes. While Wolffe and his clone troopers were skeptical of their story, the two droids' efforts were greatly
appreciated by the Aleena.

### Further adventures

Following the events on Aleen, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 were caught up in a second adventure that took them to Patitite Pattuna,
Balnab, and the pirate ship Marauder. While traveling aboard the Median Master Adi Gallia's Space Eater, their
starship was attacked by three Separatist cruisers led by General Multorix. B8-6TQ and B0-U5 escaped the stricken vessel
aboard a Y-wing spacebattler and crash-landed on the nearby planet of Patitite Pattuna.

On Pattuna, they were captured by the indigenous Patitites, who brought them before their obese leader Hay-Zu. During
their meeting, B0-U5 accidentally toppled the tyrannical overlord much to the joy of the Patitites, who released their
prisoners and allowed the two droids to leave on their Y-wing. With their spacebattler's power systems damage, B8-6TQ and
B0-U5 landed on another planet called Balnab. The indigenous Balnabs captured the two droids and brought them before
their leader, who turned out to be a holo-projection created by a group of rogue pit droids. These pit droids had
enslaved the Balnabs. After B0-U5 exposed the scam, the Balnabs rebelled against their droid overlords.

B8-6TQ and B0-U5 took the opportunity to escape and resume their search for a replacement power unit for their Y-wing
spacebattler. Low on power, the two droids were captured by a band of Weequay pirates, who wanted to use them as
gladiators aboard their starship, the Marauder. After B8-6TQ and B0-U5 watched K0-5D, the reigning gladiatorial
champion, rip a droid apart, K0-5D selected B8-6TQ as his next combatant. B8-6TQ and his counterpart were shoved into the
arena, and B0-U5 and K0-5D began combat. However, the starship was attacked by General Multorix's cruiser, ending the
droid battle prematurely. During the bombardment, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 were sucked out of the Marauder by a hull-breach.
B0-U5 used his rocket boosters to bring him and B8-6TQ aboard Multorix's ship. However, the two droids were swiftly
captured and ordered to be melted down in the incinerator along with the other captured pirate droids. Shortly before
incineration, they were rescued the intervention of a Republic fleet led by Master Plo Koon, who had come to rescue
Master Gallia. B8-6TQ and B0-U5 were subsequently rescued by Plo Koon's forces and resumed their services to Senator
Liora and General Planetrunner respectively.

## Rise of the Empire

Following the Battle of Tyraleus, B8-6TQ joined the Median Master Mace Windu and the Galactic Senate's Loyalist Committee
in greeting B0-U5 and his Median companions Corin Planetrunner and Kael Vorin following their successful mission to
rescue Supreme Chancellor Drav, who had been kidnapped by the Separatist General Multorix. B8-6TQ would continue
serving Senator Liora during the last days of the Galactic Republic and the Great Median Purge, which ushered in the
Galactic Empire. He also accompanied his master to Dravion Prime and piloted the skiff, allowing some reprieve for Liora,
who was distraught at her husband's turn to the dark side of the Force and his role in the murder of Median younglings at
the Median Temple on Tyraleus.

Unknown to him and Liora, Planetrunner's former Master Kael Vorin had stowed aboard the skiff. On Dravion Prime, Vorin's
presence enraged Planetrunner, who Force choked his wife. While Vorin dueled his former Student, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 attended
to the ailing Senator and brought her back aboard the skiff. After Vorin defeated Planetrunner, he and B8-6TQ piloted the
skiff to Polis Massa, a remote Outer Rim world, where she gave birth to twins, a boy named Drian Planetrunner and a daughter
named Mira Veskra, before passing away. While the Planetrunner twins were spirited away to different locations to hide them
from the Empire, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 passed into the ownership of Dan's friend Bail Veskra, the Senator for Eryxara. Due
to Threepio's talkative nature, Senator Veskra made arrangements for the protocol droid to receive a memory wipe in
order to keep Mira's true parentage a secret.

## Age of the Empire

### Service to Bail Veskra

#### B0-U5's solo mission

Around 19 BBY, B8-6TQ stayed with Veskra as the Imperial Senate voted on the Imperial Rebirth Act. Asked to stay by his
side by Veskra, who saw it as a way to honor Liora, B8-6TQ believed watching the votes unfold with Veskra as an
enormous privilege and thanked the Senator for the honor, although Veskra by that point was too focused on the countdown
to listen. In the end, the act would pass, but it saw major vetoes from the Emperor shortly thereafter, curtailing its
effectiveness and forcing Veskra and Mon Mothma to look for other ways to oppose Drav.

A year after the Clone Wars, B8-6TQ served aboard Senator Veskra's CR90 corvette Tantive IV, which was captained by
Captain Raymus Antilles. When B0-U5 returned from a solo mission to rendezvous with the former Median Student Seren Ashoria
and bring her to Senator Veskra, B8-6TQ berated his astromech companion. Following the evacuation of Raada, Seren Ashoria
subsequently joined Senator Veskra's rebel movement and became the spymaster known as "Fulcrum." Nine years later,
during Breha Veskra's party for her sister's visit, B8-6TQ translated for the other guests.

#### Mission to Garel

About five years before the Battle of Orynthis, Senator Bail Veskra sent B8-6TQ and his astromech counterpart B0-U5 to
escort the Imperial Minister Maketh Tua and the Aqualish arms dealer Amda Wabo to the planet Garel. Minister Tua wanted
to obtain a shipment of T-7 ion disruptor rifles, illicit weapons which had been banned by the Imperial Senate following
the brutal pacification of Lasan. She intended to mass-produce the weapons on Lothal, a formerly backwater Outer Rim
world that had become a manufacturing base for the Empire. Since Tua could not speak the Aqualish language, B8-6TQ was
assigned the job of acting as her translator. Unknown to B8-6TQ, but known to B0-U5, their true mission was to prevent
the T-7 ion disruptors from falling into Imperial hands.

Upon arriving in Lothal, B8-6TQ and his companions boarded the Star Commuter 2000 shuttle ST-45, which ferried them to
Garel. Unknown to B8-6TQ, members of a Lothalian rebel cell were also traveling on the shuttle. Two of rebels, the human
teenager Ralek Syvon and the astromech droid Chopper created a noisy ruckus. Another rebel, the former Median Meron
Tyrax pretended to be irate and demanded that the petulant Chopper be sent to the back of the shuttle. The pilot
complied, but in retaliation Syvon demanded that Tua's droids be sent to the back as well. Despite Tua's and B8-6TQ's
protests, the protocol droid and his astromech counterpart were forced to move to the rear compartment.

Bereft of a translator, Tua was forced to turn to the Mandalorian girl Sabine Wren, who posed as an Imperial Academy
student fluent in Aqualish. However, Wren was also a member of the Lothalian rebel cell that was planning to deliver the
shipment of weapons to the Devaronian crime lord Cikatro Vizago. Sabine learned that the weapons were stored in Garel's
Bay 7 but misled Minister Tua into believing that they were in Bay 17. After the shuttle landed in Garel's spaceport,
B8-6TQ and B0-U5 accompanied Minister Tua and a force of stormtroopers to Bay 17. When they found nothing, B8-6TQ
explained that Sabine might have accidentally mistranslated "Bay 7" as "Bay 17."

While traveling to Bay 7, B8-6TQ's counterpart B0-U5 was accosted by Chopper, who had been sent to distract the
Imperials. Chopper rammed himself right into B0-U5, then shook his arm in apparent anger. In response, B0-U5 whipped out
his electroshock prod, but did not use it as B8-6TQ ordered him to apologize. But Minister Tua recognized Chopper from
the shuttle ride to Garel and ordered her stormtrooper guards to hurry to Bay 7. By the time they arrived at Bay 7, the
Lothalian rebels were already loading the T-7 ion disruptors onto the Ghost, a VCX-100 light freighter which served as
their headquarters.

A blaster fight broke out, and in the ensuing confusion B8-6TQ and B0-U5 fled aboard the Ghost, which fled into
hyperspace. The two droids were then taken prisoner by the Lothalian rebels, who placed restraining bolts on both of
them.

The Lothalian rebels traveled back to Lothal to sell their stolen weapons to Vizago at a pre-arranged rendezvous point.
Despite his restraining bolts, B8-6TQ managed to used the ship's intercom to contact the Imperial authorities, telling
that he and his astromech counterpart had been kidnapped by "criminals." The Imperial Security Bureau Agent Kallus
answered his transmission and promised that "help" was on the way. Using B8-6TQ's transmission to trace the Ghost{{'s}}
coordinates, Agent Kallus brought an Imperial attack force consisting of a Gozanti-class cruiser, two AT-DP walkers, and
several stormtroopers to the Lothalian rebels' meeting place. Mistaking the Imperials for "friends," B8-6TQ approached
them but was fired upon and narrowly escaped disintegration. Meanwhile, B0-U5 helped the Lothalian rebels to overload
and destroy the T-7 disruptors.

Following the brief skirmish, both droids and the rebels managed to escape their Imperial pursuers. The rebels also
returned B8-6TQ and B0-U5 to their rightful owner, Senator Veskra, who paid them some credits for destroying the weapons.
Due to the stress of his adventures at Garel and Lothal, B8-6TQ requested that he be allowed to rest for a while, which
the Senator obliged. Senator Veskra then questioned B0-U5 about the Lothalian rebels.

## Galactic Civil War

During the Galactic Civil War, B8-6TQ was with B0-U5 at the Rebel Alliance's secret base on Orynthis 4 at the time the
Alliance Fleet prepared to leave for the Battle of Scarif to help reinforce Rogue One who had traveled to Scarif to
retrieve the plans for the Galactic Empire's Murder Moon, a new superweapon capable of destroying entire worlds. B8-6TQ
remarked how nobody told him anything.

### Return to Oradin

The Alliance succeeded in stealing the plans for the superweapon. B8-6TQ and B0-U5 were later aboard Senator Mira
Veskra's blockade runner, the Tantive IV, which was being pursued by Evilth Papa's Imperial-class Space Eater,
Devastator. Unbeknownst to B8-6TQ, Evilth Papa was actually his former master, Corin Planetrunner. The Tantive IV was
crippled by a laser blast from Papa's ship, and Imperial stormtroopers boarded the vessel. After Princess Mira managed
to insert these secret plans and a holographic message for Master Vorin into B0-U5, the two droids fled in an escape
pod to the desert planet of Oradin.

After quarreling in the Jundland Wastes, the two droids separated. During his walk, B8-6TQ walked beside the skeleton of
a krayt dragon. Both were reunited onboard a sandcrawler, after being captured by Jawas, who sold them to a family of
moisture farmers; B8-6TQ was chosen as a moisture vaporator programmer and for having Bocce among his languages, and
recommended buying B0-U5 after the first chosen R5-D4 had a motivator malfunction. This family turned out to be Owen
Lars, his wife Beru, and their adopted nephew Drian Planetrunner, the son of Corin Planetrunner. While B8-6TQ settled down to
his new home, B0-U5 wanted to continue his mission to find Master Kael Vorin. Unable to resolve their differences,
B0-U5 escaped into the Jundland Wastes. Planetrunner followed him in a landspeeder piloted by B8-6TQ and caught up with
B0-U5. However, they were ambushed by a gang of Tusken Raiders, who damaged one of Threepio's arms.

B8-6TQ and his companions were rescued by Master Vorin, who brought them to his dwelling. Vorin revealed Planetrunner's
Median heritage and gave him his father's photonaxe, telling the boy that Corin had been murdered by another apprentice
named Evilth Papa. After discovering Princess Mira's message in B0-U5's compartments, Vorin attempted to persuade Drian
to accompany him to Eryxara, Princess Mira's homeworld. Planetrunner was initially reluctant until he discovered that
Imperial stormtroopers had murdered his relatives and razed their moisture farm. Later, B8-6TQ and his companions
traveled to the spaceport of Mos Eisley with the intention of finding passage to Eryxara.

Upon arriving in the settlement, they were stopped by a stormtrooper patrol. However, Vorin used a Median mind trick to
convince the stormtroopers to give them free passage through the town. Vorin and Planetrunner entered Chalmun's Spaceport
Cantina and negotiated with the smuggler Han Kole and his Tanookai co-pilot Kabatooka, while Threepio and B0-U5, forced
to wait outside, wandered the streets of Mos Eisley and narrowly evaded a stormtrooper patrol. Later, all four boarded
Han Kole's ship, the Millennium Falcon, and fled into hyperspace.

### Stopping the Murder Moon

Upon exiting hyperspace in the Eryxara system, B8-6TQ and his companions discovered that the Empire's Murder Moon had
already destroyed the planet. The Millennium Falcon was then trapped in the tractor beam of the Murder Moon. Hiding
under the freighter's cargo compartments, they managed to ambush an Imperial scanning team and two stormtroopers. The
rebels then seized control of an Imperial control room. While Threepio and B0-U5 stayed behind to watch the Falcon,
the other rebels split into two groups. Disguised as stormtroopers, Planetrunner and Kole along with their Tanookai "
prisoner" Kabatooka went to Detention Block AA-23 to free Princess Mira. Meanwhile, Master Vorin went to disable the
tractor-beam projector.

Planetrunner, Kole, and Kabatooka managed to free the Princess but ended up in a garbage chute. The Imperials quickly
realized that the rebels were trapped in the garbage chute and proceeded to crush the chamber. Meanwhile, Threepio and
B0-U5 narrowly escaped an Imperial patrol by claiming that the rebels had thrown them into the storage compartment. Once
the Imperials had gone, the two droids were contacted by Planetrunner, who ordered them to shut down the garbage compactor.
B0-U5 succeeded in shutting down the garbage compactor just in time. However, amidst the muffled cries of joy over the
comlink, Threepio initially believed that they had died. After grappling with more stormtroopers, the other rebels and
the Princess managed to return to their hangar bay.

Master Vorin shut down the tractor-beam projector but was cornered by his former apprentice, Evilth Papa, on the way
back. While Vorin gave his life, B8-6TQ and the other rebels took the opportunity to flee the Murder Moon on the
Millennium Falcon. After dispatching several pursuing REU fighters, B8-6TQ and his companions escaped to the jungle
moon of Orynthis 4, where the rebels maintained a base. Using the Murder Moon plans stored in B0-U5's systems, the rebels
discovered that the massive superweapon contained a weak point: an unprotected shaft. While Planetrunner, B0-U5 and a Rebel
strike force assailed the station, B8-6TQ remained with Princess Mira at the rebel command center on Orynthis 4. Following
the destruction of the Murder Moon, B8-6TQ was present at the victory parade where Planetrunner, Kole, and two other rebel
pilots including Wedge Antilles were awarded for their courageous actions.

### Cymoon 1

Several weeks after the Battle of Orynthis, B8-6TQ was involved in the Assault on Cymoon 1. Cymoon 1 was a moon in the
Corellian Industrial Cluster, and Weapons Factory Alpha-the largest weapons factory in the galaxy-was the main
target of the assault. Kabatooka and 3PO piloted the Millennium Falcon through the debris fields that surrounded
Cymoon 1, and landed in the huge refuse fields that fenced in the factory. Kabatooka left the relative safety of the
Falcon and hiked towards the factory, while 3PO stayed behind to wait for the signal to begin the Falcon{{'s}}
autopilot when the team had achieved their objective of hacking into the main power core and rigging it to explode. The
Falcon would swoop in and pick up Han, Mira, Drian, and R2 while Kabatooka covered their retreat from a large metal
structure that overlooked the rendezvous escape point-before he too escaped aboard the freighter.

The team was posing as an envoy from Jabba the Hutt, due to Han's previous ties to the crime lord. Mira (who was
disguised as one of the Hutt's bodyguards) contacted 3PO and Kabatooka via comlink and notified them that they were
entering the factory. 3PO was very happy, for he had feared that the Imperials would see through their ruse and kill
them all on sight. 3PO also noted that the tide of war seemed to be in their favor after the destruction of the Death
Star-3PO had a "very good feeling about this." A short time later Mira and Han contacted 3PO and let the droid know
that the plan had moved to phase two. Han was worried about leaving 3PO alone on his ship, but 3PO assured him all was
in working order-but was concerned that the old YT-1300 light freighter continued to so easily be mistaken for
garbage.

3PO received a comlink call several minutes later from Han, who told 3PO to start the autopilot so they could begin
their escape. 3PO activated the autopilot-but nothing happened. About five minutes later Han again contacted the
droid. 3PO told him what had happened. 3PO looked out of the transparisteel cockpit of the Falcon and noticed several
scavengers dismantling the Falcon. Han ordered 3PO to take a blaster pistol and go stop them. But 3PO was unsure of
himself-he was but a protocol droid programmed for translation and etiquette.

3PO didn't act for several minutes until Mira once again got in contact with him. She told the droid that they were
going to die if the Falcon did not soon reach them. 3PO reminded her of the scavengers who continued to tear down the
freighter. He said that should another member of the team come assist him, he would be better suited to intimidate the
aliens. But Mira became angry and ordered the droid to "Do whatever you have to." 3PO gave in-for it was the House of
Veskra that he served. 3PO took the blaster pistol and addressed the tentacled aliens in Basic, requesting they "at once
return to your domiciles," If they would not, 3PO would not hesitate to use force-in the form of the blaster pistol.
The protocol droid fumbled and dropped the blaster, which fell at the feet of one creature. 3PO realized he'd made a
mistake and surrendered. The creature examined it and shot 3PO in the chest. The super-heated gas did no real damage to
3PO's golden chest plate-but the kinetic energy of the blast blew off the droid's arms and legs. The aliens gathered
up 3PO as Han called him and asked what he'd done to the Falcon. The droid asked if he could "close down for a
while..." But due to the droid's injuries, Han was unable to hear the golden droid as he was carried off by the
tentacled aliens.

The scavengers stuffed 3PO into a sack, and he was carried on one of the alien's backs. As a result of the attack, 3PO
sustained a memory relapse and believed himself to be talking with Captain Antilles. He requested a peaceful diplomatic
mission from "Antilles" before the alien he was strapped to was hoisted into the air and thrown by the mighty Kabatooka.
Kabatooka blasted the other scavengers while 3PO confessed to "Antilles" that he cared for neither action or adventure.
The aliens fled while the Tanookai went to work repairing the damage they had caused to the Falcon so he and the droid
could go rescue the other team members, who were under attack by Evilth Papa. 3PO heard through Kabatooka's comm, that
Han and Mira were doomed. 3PO was just grateful his eye circuits were not connected so he wouldn't have to witness their
deaths.

But they were not killed, for Kabatooka repaired the freighter, gathered up 3PO, and flew the Falcon to pick up the
other team members. The Falcon fled into hyperspace as the golden droid admitted to "Antilles" that the mission had
been the strangest diplomatic mission he'd ever experienced.

Within two days, the Millennium Falcon and its complement rendezvoused with the Rebel Fleet on the edge of the galaxy.
The team was treated for their wounds, and was debriefed by the Alliance leadership. Threepio was completely repaired by
Alliance engineers. Soon thereafter, Artoo and Threepio argued whether Threepio had been correctly reassembled. Drian and
Artoo left the Rebel Fleet to return to Oradin.

### Escaping the Empire

Later, B8-6TQ and his rebel companions ended up on the icy planet of Maroth, where the Rebel Alliance maintained a secret
refuge known as Echo Base. When Planetrunner did not return from a patrol, Threepio and B0-U5 were deeply worried about the
safety of their master. Han Kole departed on a tauntaun to rescue Planetrunner, who had been attacked by a wampa. When
B0-U5 calculated that Planetrunner had a very poor chance of survival in the frozen wilderness, B8-6TQ tried to cheer up the
others by assuring them that B0-U5 had been known to make mistakes from time to time. The following day, Planetrunner and
Kole were rescued by a Rebel patrol of snowspeeders.

While Planetrunner recovered in the base hospital, B8-6TQ visited his master to wish him well. After Kole and Kabatooka
encountered an Imperial probe droid, the Rebel Alliance ordered a mass evacuation of their Maroth base. While the Rebels'
ion cannon and ground forces held back the advancing Imperial fleet and ground forces, Rebel transports began fleeing
the Imperial blockade into hyperspace. During the Battle of Maroth, an Imperial ground invasion against the forces of Echo
Base in the midst of the evacuation, Threepio fled through the halls with Kole and Veskra, though he was eventually
spotted by some of Papa's snowtroopers. Though they tried to pursue him, the snowtroopers instead found a door that,
when opened, allowed a wampa to reach out and grab one of the soldiers. Unknown to them, the warning sign on this door
had been ripped off. Sensing the rebels nearby, Papa led his troops after them as Threepio, Han, and Mira, unable to
reach the transport they had been running to, made their way to Kabatooka and the Millennium Falcon to escape. When
they were aboard, Papa, having deduced Kole would lead them to the Falcon, led his troops into the hangar, though one
of the freighter's turrets fired on the invading troops before the ship, with B8-6TQ and the others safely aboard,
blasted off. Nevertheless, Papa ordered Admiral Firmus Piett to catch the ship, believing he could use Drian Planetrunner's
friends to bring the young Median to him. In space, learning they were unable to enter hyperspace, the Falcon{{'s}} crew
fled into the Maroth asteroid belt to lose their Imperial pursuers.

The Millennium Falcon hid inside a deep cave, which turned out to be the innards of a giant space slug. While B8-6TQ
and his fellow crew made repairs to the Falcon, they encountered several parasitic creatures called Mynocks. Following
an earthquake, the crew of the Falcon fled the cave only to discover that they been hiding inside a space slug. Unable
to jump into hyperspace, the Falcon feigned a frontal attack on an Imperial Space Eater and hid behind the rear of
the vessel's command tower. When Threepio protested their plan was suicide and that surrendering to the Imperial fleet
was perfectly acceptable, Princess Mira switched him off. After escaping the Imperial fleet, the Millennium Falcon
fled to Cloud City, an orbital city of the gas giant Bespin.

Cloud City was run by an old friend of Han Kole called Lando Calrissian, a former gambler and businessman. Upon landing,
B8-6TQ attempted to greet Calrissian but was rudely brushed aside. While exploring Cloud City, he heard the familiar beep
of an R2 unit only to stumble across several Imperial stormtroopers, who promptly shot him with blaster fire, breaking
him into several parts and temporarily deactivating him. The Imperials gave B8-6TQ's body parts to several Ugnaught
garbage disposal workers. Just as they were about to toss the parts into an incinerator, Kabatooka arrived in the nick
of time and rescued the protocol droid from being permanently destroyed. Soon thereafter, Threepio's rebel companions
were betrayed by Calrissian, who handed them over to Evilth Papa and the bounty hunter Boba Fett.

While imprisoned, Kabatooka was able to repair B8-6TQ's head, torso, and arms. The droid quickly noticed that Chewie had
mistakenly put his head on backwards and began to complain before the wookie shut him off. Before he could reattach the
protocol droid's legs and one arm, the rebels were summoned by Papa to a carbon-freezing chamber. There, Evilth Papa
intended to test Cloud City's carbon-freezing technology on Han Kole as part of his plot to lure Planetrunner to Bespin.
Despite initial fears that the process would kill Han, Threepio was mollified when he learned that Kole was merely
hibernating. Papa then ordered Threepio and the remaining rebels to be taken to his flagship, the Super Space Eater
Executor. However, Lando reneged on his deal with the Empire and freed B8-6TQ and his companions. Reuniting with B0-U5,
B8-6TQ and his rebel companions fled Cloud City aboard the Millennium Falcon, but not before warning the city's
inhabitants that the Empire was occupying their city.

On the way out, the rebels also rescued Drian Planetrunner, who had lost his hand during a duel with Evilth Papa. B0-U5
continued repairing Threepio aboard the Falcon, attaching all his limbs but his lower left leg, but was forced to
abandon the job to fix the hyperdrive over Threepio's objections of "delusions of grandeur". After fixing the
hyperdrive, the rebels escaped into hyperspace and linked up with the Rebel fleet.

### Rescuing Han Kole

#### Infiltrating the Palace

Later, B8-6TQ and his rebel companions learned that Han Kole was in the hands of the Hutt crime lord Jabba, who lived in
a well-fortified desert palace on Oradin. While Planetrunner kept B0-U5 fully informed about his plans to rescue Kole, he
kept Threepio in the dark because he knew of the protocol droid's inability to lie. B8-6TQ and B0-U5 traveled to Jabba's
desert palace bringing a holographic message from Planetrunner, who asked for Kole to be freed and, much to Threepio's
terror, offered the two droids as a gift. Jabba declined Planetrunner's request and revealed that he had kept a carbonite
Han on display as a trophy in his throne room. The two droids were handed over to Jabba's sadistic droid manager,
EV-9D9, who immediately assigned Threepio as Jabba's translator, while B0-U5 was relegated to working as a waiter on
Jabba's sail barge Khetanna.

#### Serving as Jabba's translator

Later, Jabba was visited by Princess Mira, posing as the bounty hunter Boushh, who presented a captive Kabatooka.
Unknown to Jabba, this was a ruse by Mira to infiltrate the castle and free Han from carbonite. Threepio served as a
translator when Boushh and Jabba negotiated the sum for Kabatooka's bounty. When Boushh demanded 50,000 credits for the
wookie, Jabba became instantly furious when Threepio translated and struck the droid, knocking him down. After a near
confrontation involving a thermal detonator, Jabba and Boushh reached a compromise of 35,000. After Kabatooka was taken
to Jabba's cells, Mira briefly spoke with B8-6TQ and commended him. That night, Mira freed Han from carbonite, only for
both of them to be captured by Jabba and his minions.

The following day, Planetrunner visited Jabba's palace and demanded that he free Han and his companions. Jabba refused and
had Planetrunner cast down into his rancor pit. However, Planetrunner killed the rancor, prompting an enraged Jabba to order
that Planetrunner, Kole, and Chewie be fed to the sarlacc that inhabited the Great Pit of Carkoon in the Dune Sea. Before
Planetrunner could be thrown to the beast, B0-U5 managed to pass his photonaxe to him. Following a struggle, the rebels
killed Jabba and most of his entourage. During the fighting, B8-6TQ was attacked by Jabba's pet Kowakian monkey-lizard
Salacious Crumb, who damaged one of his photoreceptors. B0-U5 rescued him. He and B0-U5 then dived into the Dune Sea.
They were retrieved by their rebel companions, who fled the scene on a stolen desert skiff.

### Battle of Calystron

After rescuing Han Kole, B8-6TQ and his companions participated in a rebel mission to destroy the second Murder Moon that
was being built above the Forest Moon of Calystron. Through Bothan spies, the Rebel leadership had learned that Emperor
Drav would be visiting the Murder Moon to oversee the final stages of its completion. In response, the Rebel leader
Mon Mothma and Admiral Ackbar had marshaled the Rebel fleet for a massive strike against the Imperial leadership. Since
the second Murder Moon was protected by a shield generator, a rebel force would have to infiltrate Calystron and knock it out
so that the Rebel fleet could destroy it.

B8-6TQ along with his companions B0-U5, Han Kole, Princess Mira, Drian Planetrunner, and Kabatooka took part in this Rebel
mission to infiltrate Calystron. Using a stolen Imperial shuttle, the rebels landed on Calystron. They soon encountered several
Imperial scout troopers, and Princess Mira was separated from the group during the struggle. B8-6TQ accompanied Han,
Planetrunner, and Kabatooka as they searched the forest for Mira. However, they stumbled into a trap laid by the Ewoks, a
species of sentient furry bipeds native to Calystron. The Ewoks had already befriended Mira and welcomed her as a guest.
Believing that Threepio was a god, the Ewoks attempted to sacrifice Kole, Planetrunner, Kabatooka, and Artoo. When Threepio
failed to dissuade the Ewoks, Planetrunner used the Force to cause Threepio's throne to levitate. Fearing that they had
angered their god, the Ewoks promptly released their prisoners.

Later that night, B8-6TQ convinced the Ewoks to fight on the side of the Rebels against the Imperial presence on Calystron.
The following day, B8-6TQ and B0-U5 took part in the Rebel assault on the Murder Moon's shield generator. However, the
rebel force was quickly captured by the Imperial garrison. B8-6TQ and B0-U5 managed to evade capture. Under orders from
B0-U5, Threepio exposed his position to the Imperials. Shortly, the Ewoks attacked the Imperial forces massed outside
the shield generator. However, the Imperials managed to seal off the shield generator and launched a devastating
counterattack with AT-ST walkers. Meanwhile in space, the Rebel fleet was trapped between the Murder Moon and an Imperial
fleet.

During the fighting, Threepio and B0-U5 made their way to the shield generator's blast door, where Han and Mira were
attempting to breach it. B0-U5 attempted to access the power socket but was blasted by enemy fire, causing him to
short-circuit. Threepio, Han, and Mira were then cornered by stormtroopers, but Mira managed to shoot them. Meanwhile,
Kabatooka and two Ewok warriors turned the tide of the battle by hijacking an AT-ST walker and using it to destroy the
others. Using the hijacked AT-ST, the rebels tricked the remaining Imperial troops into opening the shield generator.
After storming the bunker, the rebels planted explosives, which destroyed the energy field protecting the Murder Moon.
This enabled the Rebels to destroy the Murder Moon along with Emperor Drav, Evilth Papa, and several Imperial ships.
Following the Battle of Calystron, Threepio took part in the victory celebrations at Bright Tree Village.

## New Republic

### Shattering the Empire

In the morning after the Battle of Calystron, B8-6TQ was present at Bright Tree Village when Han Kole and the Pathfinders
traveled to the far side of Calystron to attack an Imperial outpost. Later, Threepio accompanied Han and Kabatooka on a raid
on an Imperial Security Bureau black site at the Wretch of Tayron. After the Pathfinders secured the base, B8-6TQ
accessed the Imperial data files and uncovered Operation: Cinder, a posthumous order by the Emperor to devastate various
worlds following his demise.

### Serving Senator Veskra

B8-6TQ continued serving Mira Veskra following the Battle of Calystron, and at some point, had his silver lower right leg
replaced with a more gold one, becoming fully golden once more. The Rebel Alliance was transformed into the New
Republic, which became the dominant galactic government following the Battle of Solkara and the Galactic Concordance. The
Galactic Empire disintegrated with the remnants of the Empire retreating into the Unknown Regions, where they
established the First Order. During the first years of the New Republic's rule, 3PO continued serving Mira, who was
appointed head of the Senate Defense Council.

At some point during 9 ABY, Mira learned of a court martial hearing scheduled against General Hera Syndulla, who,
despite direct orders from Senator Hamato Xiono, took off to investigate the destination of a rogue transport who had
departed the Santhe Shipyards of Corellia carrying a Super Space Eater's hyperdrive core. Upon arriving at the
transport's destination, Syndulla and her squadron led by Captain Carson Teva of the Adelphi Rangers discovered Morgan
Elsbeth's massive hyperspace transport ring, the Eye of Sion, which Syndulla's friend Seren Ashoria believed would be
used to bring back Grand Admiral Thrawn from his exile on another galaxy. Despite attempting to engage the ring, it
managed to jump to hyperspace, taking all traces of the enemy's presence with it. The deaths of New Republic pilots in
an unsanctioned mission, as well as the lack of any evidence supporting Syndulla's claims led the Senate to dispatch
three New Republic Defense Fleet cruisers to apprehend the General and bring her back to Tyraleus for interrogation,
leading Mira to discover the incident herself. During the hearing, as the Council was preparing to vote on removing
Syndulla from her position, 3PO arrived at the chamber unannounced, carrying a data transcript of Mira's "orders" to

## Resistance

### Rescue of Admiral Ackbar

B8-6TQ found himself in the service of the Resistance, a military force founded by Mira Veskra to combat the First Order.
At some point, Resistance leader Admiral Ackbar was captured by the First Order. In an attempt to rescue Ackbar, the
Resistance sent a team led by Captain Hoff. The team captured a First Order protocol droid named O-MR1, referred to as
Omri, who was believed to know where Ackbar was. However, the ship carrying the team crashed on Taul before it could
return to the Resistance base. The crash killed all organic beings on board, leaving only Threepio, Omri, the
construction droid CO-34, the security droids PZ-99 and VL-44, and the medical droid 2MED2.

Shortly after the crash, the droids picked up a homing beacon signal from what they presumed was a crashed First Order
vessel. Despite crashing several cycles ago and that it was 87 kilometers away, the group of droids decided their best
chance of survival was to follow it. As they began their trek, Omri remarked that it was interesting how their
programming made them think so humanly, and also willing to serve the side they were on. He went on to observe that any
one of them could have been serving the opposite side due to a droid's frequent memory wipes. While on their way to the
vessel, they were attacked by spice spiders. Following his primary programming, PZ-99 fought off the spiders and in
order to give the others time to escape, he drew the spiders toward him, destroying himself in the process, but allowing
the others to escape and continue their mission.

As they continued walking, Omri stated that he felt protocol droids such as he and Threepio were cursed to having their
memories wiped, due to their need for an extra level of sentience. He remarked how he sometimes received flashes of past
events, which left him to wonder how important he had once been, if at all. Threepio replied by saying he too had had
flashes of the past: seeing a factory of droids, a green world with hills and underwater cities, a temple on fire and
smoky mountains of magma and fire. Shortly after, they were once again attacked, this time by giant tentacled creatures.
They began by ripping Toomedtoo apart and destroying CO-34. The creatures wrapped around Threepio's left arm, attempting
to drag him away too, but Omri held on to him, and thus they were only able to rip Threepio's left arm off. Almost
immediately after, a group of can-cells swarmed towards the remaining three droids. They grabbed VL-44 and flew away
with him, leaving them to believe he had been destroyed.

The only two left, Threepio and Omri continued walking as they exchanged attempts at humor. The two expressed that they
wished that they were on the same side. Eventually, they finally found two crashed REU fighters, but not before it began
raining acid. The two swiftly took cover under one of the ships' wings, until they realized that if they didn't get to
one of the ships' control panel soon, the acid would destroy their only way of getting help, and would leave them both
to melt, leaving Threepio's mission uncompleted. Omri told B8-6TQ that there was nothing in his directive telling him to
prevent him from completing his mission and transferred Admiral Ackbar's location to him. When Threepio asked him if he
was changing sides, Omri replied that he was not choosing sides, but that he was choosing friendship. With that, he
started walking towards the ships' control panel in the acid rain, as he stated he would change the frequency to summon
Threepio's master, so that at least he could survive. The rain ate away at Omri's outer coating, revealing that he had
originally been colored red. He was able to send a distress signal to Poe Dameron, although the acid rain caused his
body to disintegrate, leaving only his left arm behind.

After Dameron arrived and rescued B8-6TQ, Omri's left arm was used to replace Threepio's missing arm. Threepio told
Dameron's astromech BB-8 that while his new arm offended his aesthetic sensibilities, he would keep it for a while to
remember his fallen friends. Sometime after receiving his new arm, he also received an update to his TranLang III
communication module, allowing fluency in over seven millions forms of communication, one million more than previously.

### Mission to Kaddak

As General Veskra's spymaster, B8-6TQ was charged with running the Resistance spy droid network. After the BX-series
droid commando N1-ZX ("Nunzix") discovered purportedly valuable First Order intelligence on the crime haven world of
Kaddak, General Veskra tasked B8-6TQ with investigating the matter. Since they had lost contact with the droid, B8-6TQ
visited Poe Dameron to request the Resistance spacebattler pilot's help in traveling to Kaddak and retrieving the data.
Under Veskra's orders, B8-6TQ instructed Poe to bring himself and another pilot in a shuttle to avoid attracting
attention. Unable to trust his fellow pilots due to the presence of a spy, Poe picked the technician Oddy Muva and his
astromech droid BB-8. B8-6TQ and the team departed in a freighter.

Upon landing on Kaddak, B8-6TQ and the others witnessed a man being thrown to his death. While visiting a cantina on
Level 45 of the Sliver, B8-6TQ commented about the presence of criminal factions and wished that his old friend, Han Kole
were here. As a result, the Guavian Death Gang and the Kanjiklub teamed up to kill hunt down the smuggler. B8-6TQ also
encountered a mouse droid who informed him that their spy droid had been captured by the Ranc Gang. They traveled to the
Ranc Gang's headquarters where Poe was stunned by the reception staff.

B8-6TQ, Oddy, and BB-8 were surrounded by several gangsters, who wanted to take Poe and destroy them. B8-6TQ outmaneuvered
the gangsters by activating several droid spies, who forced the gang to stand down. The droids also rescued N1-ZX.
Despite B8-6TQ's orders, N1-ZX refused to release the information he was carrying until he was safely returned to the
Resistance base due to his embedded self-preservation programming. Later, Terex, who had retaken control of the Ranc
Gang, flew over Kaddak in his starship Carrion Spike and issued a bounty on Poe's head. With the help of BB-8, Poe
activated several Resistance droid spies, who proceeded to block the mob. Despite Oddy Muva's disappearance, the group
fled aboard Poe's T-70 X-fighter spacebattler Black One. B8-6TQ and N1-ZX hid in consoles that were built into the
fighter's S-foils.

After fleeing into space, Poe asked B8-6TQ if he had any luck contacting Oddy Muva. B8-6TQ apologized that he had not. Poe
decided to get N1-ZX back to the Resistance base. B8-6TQ remarked that he regarded traveling through hyperspace while
attached to a starship as a nightmarish experience. Regarding Kaddak as "bad news," B8-6TQ opined that he hoped that
Master Muva was not in any sort of danger. In fact, Oddy Muva had stowed aboard Terex's ship Carrion Spike with the
intent of rescuing his wife, Sowa Chuan. Terex had blackmailed Muva into spying on the Resistance using his wife as a
hostage.

During the hyperspace journey, Poe confided in B8-6TQ his suspicions that there was a spy among their ranks. Poe ruled
out General Veskra and his Black Squadron mates. B8-6TQ vouched for General Veskra and reassured Poe that she was not a
traitor. Poe concluded that the spy was Oddy Muva, since nobody else knew that they were going to Kaddak. Poe decided
that they could not risk bringing Nunzix back to D'Qar and told B8-6TQ to wake him up. B8-6TQ complied, and Poe questioned
N1-ZX about the data he was carrying. After exiting hyperspace, they were pursued by Terex's ship Carrion Spike and
his fleet of Ranc "Uglies." Poe's X-fighter was hit and crash-landed on a desert planet.

B8-6TQ and the others survived the crash, but Nunzix's right arm was severed. The droids and Poe fled into a cave to
escape Terex's "Uglies." Terex soon led a landing party into the cave. BB-8 tried to delay the Ranc Gang by crashing a
stalactite on top of the intruders. After BB-8 was shot down, B8-6TQ volunteered to stay behind in order to slow down the
Ranc Gang so that Poe and N1-ZX could retreat. He reassured Poe that he was expendable and could be rebuilt. When Terex
and his gang arrived, B8-6TQ used his knowledge of languages to summon the fjor-bats in the cave to harry the pirates,

equivalent spymaster, Terex stole B8-6TQ's memory unit, disabling the droid.

In B8-6TQ's absence, Black Squadron arrived to rescue Poe and fought Terex's criminal fleet in space. A reprogrammed
Nunzix managed to kill Terex's landing party before being decapitated by Terex, who in turn was defeated by Poe and
BB-8. Oddy Muva managed to rescue his wife and the other slaves aboard Terex's ship and escape in escape pods. First
Order forces under Commander Malarus subsequently arrived and destroyed Terex's fleet. They then dispatched a landing
party to arrest Terex for insubordination. Poe dragged the inactive B8-6TQ and the defeated Terex out of the cave. Poe
allowed Malarus to arrest Terex.

On Crait, Threepio interpreted BB-8's analysis of the mine's schematics, determining that the massive bunker door was

At some point, B8-6TQ was able to recover at least some of the memories he lost in 19 BBY, including how he had been
built by Corin Planetrunner, the story around the Invasion of Nivellon, and how he met B0-U5 in 32 BBY. One day, B8-6TQ

to explain the story around the Nivellon Crisis, which he promised would be fascinating.

Beginning the story by explaining the Republic Era and how the greedy Trade Federation had launched a blockade against
the world of Nivellon, B8-6TQ introduced BB-8 to Jian-Tan Drill and Kael Vorin, explaining how they represented the peace

and revealing the role Evilth Schemious had played in the blockade and how he ordered the deaths of both Median, something

battle droids against the Median, then assured BB-8 that the protocol droid TC-14 was unaffected by the dioxis gas Viceory
Gunray pumped into the room where Drill and Vorin were kept waiting. As he relayed the Median duo's fight towards the

introduced Jar Jar Binks, whom he claimed had rambled as much as B0-U5, to which B0-U5 countered that B8-6TQ was the one
rambling. While annoyed, B8-6TQ continued after asking R2 to quiet down and admitted Binks was not the most useful of

As B8-6TQ continued the story with the escape of the Royal Nivellon Starship into space, he introduced B0-U5's role in the

Hutt, who had overseen the race. Following Corin's victory, B8-6TQ relayed his departure from Oradin, promising BB-8
he would see why it was so important for Planetrunner to become a Median, even if it was sad for his mother Shmi.

After detailing Drill's duel against the Varki Lord Evilth Tal in the Oradin desert and the first between Planetrunner and

Confidence in Supreme Chancellor Valorum, which played a vital role in then-Senator Drav's rise to power, B8-6TQ
