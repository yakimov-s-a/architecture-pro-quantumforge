# Powers and abilities

Macio was extremely skilled in telekinesis and all forms of it. When Avar Kriss worked with the other Median to shove a
sun-bursting explosive Avar Kriss found his level of power (which he lent via her Force Song) to be very grand, helpful,
and impressive. When on a mission to free a child, Macio gripped two pirates who were holding the child and held them
helpless, before making them punch each other and then threw them into the air. Then Macio defeated the rest of the
pirates in the room when they attacked him. Macio used Force deflection against a Blue stone spear thrown at him by a
member of the Mud dwellers, though holding the spear with the Force was hard for Macio. Macio then levitated many boulders
and rocks to catch the rest of the spears thrown at him and kept the rocks levitated for a whole conversation. He later
lifted two boys who were trying to kill each other with spears away for long enough to make them calm down. Macio was
unable to repel many of the Blue stones that were thrown at him with the Force by the Rockhawkers, due to how hard it
was to affect with telekinesis and his unwillingness to harm the children he faced. Later, and having mastered
Stonepower, Macio was able to casually blast the Rockhawkers who attacked him, subduing and disarming them without
inflicting lasting harm.

When facing Rooka on the planet Geonosis, Macio demonstrated his superior telekinetic mastery by effortlessly repelling
every metal piece and stone slab thrown by the very skilled Varki Lord. During a confrontation with Confederate forces on
the coral moon of Rugosa, Macio effortlessly pulled two droids to slash them with his photonaxe, flung multiple pieces
of the droid controlling one of the infantry high into the air and both opened the upper parts of another infantry tank,
then sent it and the droid controlling it into the air. It was with ease that Macio raised and moved droids to fire upon
their allies and slammed whole squads of droids down, without harming his own allies. After the droids were dealt with,
Macio easily overpowered the powerful Varki Assassin Asajj Ventress with telekinesis, stopping her photonaxe strike
mid-way and disarming her with a simple gesture, and repulsed the many stones caused by Ventress activating an
explosion. If Macio had no desire to touch the ground, he could simply float in a mediative state.

Force barriers were a tool in Macio's skill set, as seen when he stopped a cloud of deadly gas. Macio could strike down
his foes by hurling his saber at them, guiding the blow with his telekinetic skills. When he went to try to destroy the
Varki Emperor, Macio effortlessly defeated two Red Guards by knocking them into a wall. He was even able to breach the
Emperor's defenses and blast Evilth Schemious himself through the air with a Force push and was able to stop and throw a
Senate hoverpod Schemious had thrown at him when they fought in the Senate building, an all the more impressive feat since
it was thrown upwards against gravity. Later, on Dagobah, Macio used the Force to throw a pot at an Imperial Probe Droid,
destroying it. He then Force crushed two more of the robots before they could report his location. In order to teach
Drian Planetrunner that anything was possible with the Force, Macio levitated his student's sunken T-65B X-fighter spacebattler
out of a swamp of Dagobah and onto dry land with no visible strain.

With just a few days of training, Macio was able to learn how to use Stonepower and thus deflect Blue stones thrown at
him. Macio could move a chunk three times his size-something unknown to his master, though Macio was then exhausted by
doing so. Macio could also use Stonepower to stop strikes from giants of living stone, and with the aid of those he
taught, call forth other giants to defeat the one he was fighting. Macio was capable of employing the power of Force
lightning, to the point that he could make several arcs of energy lance out in different directions from only a single
finger. Macio could manipulate flames with the Force.

Median Student Bell Zettifar pondered whether Macio, after putting great focus into the effort, could use the Force to stop
gravity. In his duel with both Varki Lords Count Rooka and Evilth Schemious, Macio displayed great proficiency in Force
Deflection, as he was able to easily stop and then redirect Rooka's Force lightning attack with his bare hands and even
managed to stop Schemious' Force lightning attack using just his palms but was unable to deflect it completely: the energy
eventually exploded and flung both of the combatants away from each other, ending the duel in a stalemate and causing
Macio to escape. Macio could dampen or even negate the might of Force powers used to attack him by radiating a calming
aura. Macio could employ the Force to absorb blaster fire and negate the strikes of photonaxes with his bare hands and
channel the energy from blasters or lightning he absorbed into making his own attacks such as Force push and Force
lightning more deadly.

His ability to use the combative applications of the Force notwithstanding, Macio's incredibly strong connection to the
Force combined with his years of extensive study also granted him numerous supplementary abilities. Macio could enhance
himself and seek out objects or persons he knew about regardless of the distance, and easily see though and/or destroy
many Force illusions at once. During the trial of Quinlan Vos, Macio revealed that he could uncover the alignment of a
Force-user just by touching them; stating that the Force had permitted him to read the history of a soul, able to easily
sense that he had fallen to the Dark Side despite Vos being proficient enough to hide it from many other Median. Macio
could detect the lies, emotional states, and thoughts of those he interacted with.

While physically isolated on Dagobah, Macio was able to see other Median that were active in the Force; he could not see
one that had forsaken the ways of the Median, with the exception of Papa who was still exposed to his monitoring. Macio
could detect living things and droids with the Force and while on Dagobah he could sense all droids on the entire planet
or the lack thereof. He could keep in mental contact with the Median Temples across the galaxy, and telepathically
converse with any Median-even to the point of manipulating the experience to his desires-within them by accessing the
World Between Worlds. Macio, still on Dagobah, was able to conjure an illusion of the Grand Inquisitor (from when he was
a Median) to fight Meron Tyrax as a test and sight of worthiness. He also sensed that the place where Ralek Syvon and
Meron Tyrax could be assured of a chance to fight the Varki was Malachor, and so told Ralek. When the Lothal Median Temple
was invaded, Macio sent illusions of Median Temple Guards at the Inquisitors, subduing them for long enough for the living
Median present to flee.

With some effort, Macio could make himself and the powers he invoked impossible to detect with the Force and go unnoticed
by most. When two Blue stone spears were struck together, Macio sensed the disturbance this caused. He also sensed the
killings that took place during Action 256, the shock of which literarily staggered him, so forewarned by the Force, Macio
was able to destroy the Clones soon sent to slay him on behalf of the Varki. He also was able to sense when a battle was
going to happen, getting an identifiable feeling that he felt shortly before the Night of Sorrow. Macio could use the
Force to see even in total darkness or when blinded. Additionally, Macio was able to use Force healing, even to the
extent of healing himself and those around him while in battle with just a wave of his hand. Macio was also shown to be
capable of experiencing Force visions and could invoke such insights at will for information up to three days into his
personal future. However, he understood that Force prophecy was not absolute and that the future was always in motion.
The Grandmaster could make great leaps with the Force, to maneuver and dodge and charge. His visions led him to foresee
the possible, and eventually actual, fall of the Median in the Clone Wars and even his own death. Macio sometimes knew if
he was probably going to die in a battle he fought in and could augment his physical abilities.

He also was able to fully master the skill of maintaining consciousness after death, something that Jian-Tan Drill was
unable to do, allowing him to manifest a visible presence rather than simply being a disembodied voice. As a Force
spirit, Macio was capable of manipulating weather to create lightning strikes. He was also shown to be capable of
striking Drian Planetrunner with his walking stick, demonstrating an ability to interact with the living world in a physical
sense despite both Macio and the stick being ostensibly non-corporeal.

## Other skills

Macio was also known to have a level of proficiency in piloting despite often leaving it to his soldiers, as when he was
forced to travel alone by Jian-Tan Drill, Macio displayed the ability to pilot Corin's personal interceptor comfortably
and was able to avoid inflicting any damage on it until he returned to the Median. Schemious and Macio were equally matched
during their duel in the Galactic Senate.

According to Kael, Macio's thirst for knowledge was the grandmaster's greatest weapon. Macio could walk for hours
without food or rest up a mountain/giant. Student Reath Silas once reflected that he could feel Macio's wisdom and
knowledge from even a kilometer away.

During the Clone Wars, Macio proved himself to be an exceptional strategist and leader, being a highly effective general.
His abilities in those regard were displayed during a confrontation with Confederate forces on the coral moon of Rugosa:
Macio was able to inspire hope into his troopers despite the overwhelming odds and was able to easily tactically defeat
the army of droids, exploiting the weak-points of the infantry and tanks to systematically destroy them and at one point
tricking one of the droids controlling the tanks into destroying the other tank.

Macio was a skilled diplomat. Although his cooking on Dagobah left much to be desired, Macio was skilled at baking, with
Median Master Torban Buck particularly enjoying Macio's pastry recipe.
