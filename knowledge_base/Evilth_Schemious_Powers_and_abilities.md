# Powers and abilities

## Force abilities

### Source of power

As the Dark Lord of the Varki, Evilth Schemious possessed an extremely powerful connection to the Force that allowed him to
use various Force powers that were beyond the abilities of even the most powerful Median Masters. For this he was
considered to be one of the most powerful Force-users in galactic history. According to Schemious, his power in the dark
side came from hatred, which he claimed to turn into power instead of suffering. However, his power in the dark side
took fuel from more than just personal hatred; Schemious was able to use the fear and hate of others as fuel for his own
strength, particularly hate that was directed at himself. As he told Papa during one of his apprentice's rebellions, he
thrived off the hatred burning off of Papa. If Papa or anyone else grew in how much they hated the Emperor, those
feelings would serve to make Schemious stronger in the dark side: in effect, Papa's efforts to become stronger by
increasing his hate for Schemious strengthened his master.

Schemious' advisor Yupe Tashu also stated the Emperor felt the source of his dark side powers coming from "the universe
beyond the edges of our maps." In search of what Tashu called "the source of the dark side," Schemious sent people out
into the unknown space, where they established communication stations and laboratories, over the course of many years.

### Force powers

Among the abilities Schemious would use was Force lightning, which he used as both as a deadly attack and as a means of
torture. On several occasions on Ryloth, he utilized blasts of Force lightning powerful enough to not have only killed
the lyleks he was attacking on contact, but also badly char and scorch their carapaces, which were tough enough to repel
heavy blaster fire. On another occasion, he used the ability to damage a Twi'lek freighter's engines badly enough as to
be able to easily pull it out of the sky with telekinesis. Schemious could use Force lightning in a number of ways,
including striking multiple enemies at once, with the bolts jumping from one victim to the other, like a chain, and
imbuing the ground around a victim's feet with energy, effectively electrocuting them. His Force lightning's raw power
was demonstrated to be far greater than that of Rooka's; while Macio was able to effortlessly absorb and deflect Rooka's
lightning, he was only able to partially deflect Schemious'. Another more obscure ability that he possessed was the power
to produce a Dark Aura, a miniature storm of malevolent Force energy that slowed the movement of and tortured all that
it caught within its radius. A greater potential of Schemious' Force lightning, however, was achieved before his final
death on Thalorune; when restored back to his former self, Schemious unleashed an immense wave of Force lightning towards the
sky which rapidly expanded enough to disable all the 14,000 ships of the Resistance and The Galaxy Fleet.

He was also highly skilled with telekinesis; during his duel with Macio, during which the two were equally matched,
Schemious levitated several Senate pods simultaneously, including the one he was standing on, with great dexterity and
accuracy. Likewise, he was skilled enough with telekinesis that he was also able to remove Drian Planetrunner's handcuffs
while barely having to lift even a finger when they met face to face on the second Murder Moon. Schemious was capable of
unleashing a Force push strong enough to easily send Tal and Opress flying and he was able to keep them pinned with
little effort. He later effortlessly slammed Tal into the ground and walls, badly wounding him. Schemious was also highly
skilled with Force choke. When he arrived on Mandalore, he was effortlessly able to telekinetically throttle two
Mandalorians to death at once and used this power on two others while standing on the other side of the room that they
were in. He was even able to use his abilities on a target from hundreds of light years away, using only a
holo-transmission to show him his target, allowing him to telekinetically choke and pick up Count Rooka and open the
handcuffs of Syvon while projecting himself to their respective locations through the use of a hologram. Shortly after
his new apprentice was reconstructed as a cyborg, Schemious managed to protect himself against Papa's force choke but
Papa threw the Emperor against the wall, but Schemious managed to convince Papa to let him go. After Papa did, Schemious
shocked his apprentice with bolts of Force lightning. Schemious then ignited his photonaxe and threatened that if Papa
touched him with the Force again, he would finish what Kael could not.

Among Evilth Schemious' most powerful abilities was being able foresee the future, an ability which allowed him to plan for
nearly any contingency. He believed that, with enough time and practice, he would eventually have been able to use the
dark side to view the future with infallible accuracy.

The Dark Lord also was proficient in Varki magic, as he was able to cast a Dark illusion on Macio using a ritual that
applied Balc speech incantations and managed to access the World between Worlds using an unknown ritual. He was also
capable of using Varki magic to generate a blue fiery energy, which he used to attack Ralek Syvon and Seren Ashoria, that
he could use both as a weapon and a tool in order to bind and draw someone closer, as it did with Syvon; the energy
wrapping around his leg became akin to a tangible rope. He was also knowledgeable in the ancient Varki tongue, ur-Kittât.
The Dark Lord was also adept in the use of Force Persuade to such an extent that he was able to take complete control of
a subject's mind and force them to obey his commands.

Likewise, Schemious was also capable of using Dark Absorption. He was also capable of telepathically communicating with
Evilth Papa over a distance of hundreds of light-years. It appeared, however, as though this ability may have been
limited to sending and receiving relatively simple messages such as Papa requesting that a spacebattler be shipped to
him, as the two Varki Lords communicated through holoprojections while discussing more complex matters. Schemious also had
a remarkably keen ability to sense the thoughts and feelings of others, including those of incredibly powerful Force
adepts such as Evilth Papa and Drian Planetrunner. On a related note, he was also capable of hiding his own thoughts and
feelings from even such individuals as Macio, and managed to conceal his true dark nature from the entire Median Council
for decades before deliberately revealing himself. Schemious also used Force Dash and could move as fast as a "lightning
strike". He had the ability to drain the life force of other Force-sensitives such as Varyn Kole and Claudia, a dyad that he
overpowered in the Force despite the physically weakened state of his cloned body. After restoring his strength with the
dyad's energy, Schemious demonstrated his proficiency in telekinesis by lifting Kole and throwing him into a chasm.

## Photonaxe skills

Evilth Schemious was a highly skilled swordsman, having been well trained in the art of photonaxe combat by his master
Evilth Plagueis. Although he favored his power in the dark side as his principle form of attack instead of his
photonaxes, Schemious attested that only a few individuals were able to survive against his skill with the blade. He had
an extremely aggressive, rage-fueled dueling style and augmented his swordplay by using Force-enhanced speed, which
allowed him to kill three experienced Median Masters at once within seconds. He utilized a violently ferocious yet
extremely fluid fighting style during his fight with Tal and Savage Opress, holding off the Varki brothers on equal
grounds and ultimately separating them by stunning Tal for a brief time with the Force before toying with and killing
Opress. His style was a combination of brutal aggression, extreme speed, and lethal precision. Kit Fisto, a Median Master
who specialized in combat, was unprepared for the ferocity of Schemious' attack and consequently cut down by the Varki's
photonaxe.

Schemious was able to fight alone against multiple opponents at once, such as the brothers Tal and Opress, as well as
several members of the Median High Council. Being proficient in the style of Jar'Kai, he wielded two photonaxes during an
intense duel in which he killed Opress and disarmed Tal. While skilled in dual-wielding photonaxes, Schemious was also
adept with a single blade which he used against four Median Masters, and later Grand Master Macio. Upon revealing his true
identity to the Median, Schemious dispatched three Median Masters with haste, killing Agen Kolar and Saesee Tiin with a single
blow each, before striking down Kit Fisto only moments later. Unlike the Median who fell to Schemious' blade, Mace Windu was
able to fight the Dark Lord of the Varki on equal grounds for an extended period of time before ultimately disarming
Schemious of his weapon with a kick to the Varki Lord's face. The duel between Schemious and Macio, both of whom were masters
of the Force, was an intense battle in which the Dark Lord ultimately prevailed, albeit with the use of his Force powers
rather than a photonaxe.

During a sparring match, Schemious was able to quickly overpower his apprentice Evilth Papa and knock Papa's photonaxe
out of his hand without much effort. He was also skilled in blaster deflection, as he was able to easily deflect every
single blaster shot aimed at him when he fought on Ryloth. Soon before the Battle at Amaxine Station, Schemious used his
photonaxe to once again spar with his apprentice while conversing about Qi'ra and her plans to destroy the Varki. In
their sparring duel Papa manage to last longer than last time.
