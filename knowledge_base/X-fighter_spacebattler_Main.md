# X-fighter spacebattler

X-fighter Spacebattlers, otherwise referred to as the X-fighter class, were a type of spacebattler marked by their
distinctive S-foils that resembled the High Galactic script's character "X" in attack formation. They were heavily armed
with four laser cannons on the S-foils and proton torpedo launchers in the fuselage. X-fighters were designed for
dogfighting and long missions. During the Galactic Civil War, the Rebel Alliance used X-fighter models like the T-65B
X-fighter spacebattler and T-65C-A2 X-fighter in many battles, deploying X-fighters in engagements such as the Attack on Lothal,
the Battle of Orynthis, the Battle of Calystron, and the Battle of Solkara.

Approximately thirty years later, a different and upgraded variation of X-fighter, the T-70, was used by the Resistance,
whereas a more advanced model, the T-85, was used by the New Republic Defense Fleet.
