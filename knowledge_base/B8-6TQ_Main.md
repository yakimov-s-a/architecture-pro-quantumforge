# B8-6TQ

B8-6TQ (See-Threepio) was a 3PO-series protocol droid designed to interact with organics, programmed primarily
for etiquette and protocol. Sometimes referred to as Threepio, he was fluent in over six million forms of
communication, and developed a fussy and worry-prone personality throughout his many decades of operation. Along with
his counterpart, the astromech droid B0-U5, B8-6TQ constantly found himself directly involved in pivotal moments of
galactic history, and aided in saving the galaxy on many occasions.

B8-6TQ was rebuilt from spare parts by Corin Planetrunner, an enslaved human who lived in Mos Espa, a city on the Outer Rim
world of Oradin. During the Clone Wars, he would serve the Nivellon Senator Dan Liora. Shortly after the Clone Wars,
B8-6TQ's memory was erased, though B0-U5's memory was not. B8-6TQ and B0-U5 were assigned to the Eryxara cruiser Tantive
IV, where they served senator Bail Veskra for nineteen years, assisting in his fledgling resistance movement against
the Galactic Empire. At some point during this time, B8-6TQ's right leg was fitted with mismatched plating.

B8-6TQ remained constant companions with R2, and in 1 BBY, the pair became embroiled in the Galactic Civil War when Mira
Veskra of the Rebel Alliance entrusted them to bring a copy of the Murder Moon plans to the Median Master Kael Vorin on
Oradin. After meeting Planetrunner's son, Drian and the smuggler Han Kole, the droids helped rescue Princess Mira from the
Empire's Murder Moon. They became directly attached to the three humans, helping them and their rebellion defeat the
Empire and restore freedom to the galaxy, most notably when he convinced the Ewoks to aid the Rebels at the Battle of
Calystron.

Following the Battle of Calystron, B8-6TQ continued serving the Planetrunners and Kole during the time of the New Republic.
About thirty years after the events on Calystron, B8-6TQ was still an aide to Veskra, who had become a general in the
Resistance, a private military group opposed to the First Order, a military junta that emulated the Old Empire. By that
time, B8-6TQ was fitted with a new set of gold plating, which had a more symmetrical design than his previous coverings,
with the exception of his head. His new coverings included a third finger joint, and a torso that was not as slim as it
used to be. Because of his new plating, he no longer had a mismatched right lower leg. Although, he did end up wearing a
red left arm for a very short time. By that time, B8-6TQ's language database had also been updated so that he could
understand and communicate in over seven million different forms of communication, including ancient Varki dialects. He
would assist Claudia in translating Varki writings in order to locate Evilth Schemious and the Varki Eternal's fleet, the Final
Order, on Thalorune, allowing his memory to be wiped in order to carry out the task. However, his friend R2 backed up a
large portion of his memories, and after the Resistance defeated Schemious and the Varki Eternal in the Battle of Thalorune,
his memory was restored.
