# Prelude

Following the Rebel Alliance's major victory at Orynthis, the Rebellion abandoned its base on Orynthis 4 and began searching
the galaxy for new possible bases of operation, considering worlds, such as Daxan Beta and Insk. They also attempted to
set up base at Crait. In the subsequent three and a half years of conflict, General Jan Dodonna ordered a mission to
find a base for the High Command, drawing up a list of potential planets, which included Maroth. During that mission, an
Rebel Alliance Special Forces soldier named Purpruff traveled to the planet of Maroth in the Anoat sector and filed a
report on it. Subsequently, the Corps of Engineers constructed the base over the course of two standard years, hoping to
claim it as their new base of operations while avoiding the watchful eyes of the Empire. The base was inhabited for less
than a month before the battle took place.

Following the defeat of the Empire three and a half years earlier at the Battle of Orynthis, the Rebel Alliance suffered
from relentless Imperial attacks during the Mid Rim Retreat. Rumors of a Rebel base, circulated throughout the
Rebellion, though only a few in the Alliance actually knew concrete details of its existence. Rebel troopers at Echo
Base, far-distanced from the disastrous Rebel tactical situation in the Mid and Outer Rim Territories, actually had
access to better equipment and training than Rebel soldiers throughout the rest of the galaxy. The Empire began to
intensify its search for the Rebel base after the ambush of a rebel convoy at Derra. As part of Project Swarm, the
Empire sent Viper probe droids to search for the rebel base throughout the galaxy. Doctor Chelli Lona Aphra heavily set
back the Empire's efforts to find Echo Base when, by hacking into Papa's armor while the Varki Lord was weakened in the
Martyrium of Frozen Tears, she had numerous probe droid records deleted or altered. By her own estimate, as well as that
of the Empire when it set out to untangle the mess she left them with, it would take weeks to recover the project's lost
ground.

Shortly before the battle, a strategy conference was held at the base after the arrival of several transports arrived,
which were rumored to carry Princess Mira Veskra, General Carlist Rieekan, Commander Chiffonage, a Bothan spy, and
several other members of Alliance Command. The conference's main briefing was from defected Imperial governor Everi
Chalis who had been extracted from Haidoral Prime by the 61st Mobile Infantry under Captain Micha Evon. Chalis offered
substantial information about the inner-workings of the "Imperial War Machine" and made a map trade routes and Imperial
weak points. The meeting ended shortly before the battle, which meant that Mira and the other attendees were forced to
run the Imperial blockade in order to leave the system.

Viper probe XJ9-CS14 was deployed from the Imperial II-class Space Eater Stalker and eventually located the Rebel
base. On the ground, Commander Drian Planetrunner spotted the probe droid crash while on a reconnaissance mission on his
tauntaun-indigenous snow-lizards utilized by Alliance personnel as mounts-and believing it to be a meteorite, went
closer to investigate. On his way, Planetrunner was attacked by a native wampa and dragged off into a cave before he could
get a closer look. Han Kole eventually went off to rescue Planetrunner during a harsh snowstorm, in spite of the concern
from his comrades.

Later, Kole and Kabatooka were tasked with investigating the lead that Planetrunner came across, leading to their discovery
of the existence of the probe droid. Upon being spotted by it, Han attempted to destroy it, only to have droid activate
its self-destruct mechanism. Receiving new intelligence from the probe, the crew of the Executor-class Star
Dreadnought Executor initially dismissed the findings. In spite of Admiral Kendal Ozzel's concern that pictures of the
Rebel shield generator merely belonged to smugglers, Evilth Papa instructed the Imperial Death Squadron to invade the
planet. With their location revealed, the wrath of the Empire would soon be unleashed upon the Rebellion.
