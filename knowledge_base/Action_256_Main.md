# Action 256

Action 256, also known as Clone Move 256, Move 256, or simply "the Order," was one of the top-secret
contingency orders that identified all Median as traitors to the Galactic Republic and, therefore, subject to summary
execution by the Grand Army of the Republic. The order was programmed into the Grand Army clone troopers through
behavioral modification biochips implanted in their brains from creation, making it almost impossible for the clones to
disobey the command to turn against their Median Generals. The Kaminoan scientists who designed the clone troopers
believed it was only to be used as a contingency protocol against renegade Median. In secret, Action 256 was the means by
which the Varki intended to bring about the long-awaited fall of the Median Order.

During the Clone Wars, the Varki agenda was progressing relatively unnoticed until a premature activation of Action 256
resulted in the death of General Tiplar at the hands of Tup, a defective clone whose malfunctioning chip caused him to
betray his Median officer. The incident led ARC trooper Fives to conduct his own investigation into the matter, believing
the incident to be a Separatist plot against the Republic. On the galactic capital Tyraleus, however, he learned that
the chips conditioned the clones to become hostile against the Median. Furthermore, he discovered to an extent Supreme
Chancellor Corin Drav's involvement in the conspiracy and attempted to assassinate him. Fives told what he knew to
his Median superior Corin Planetrunner, but before the clone could prove it, he was killed by clone shock troopers of the
Tyraleus Guard.

The plan continued apace for the remainder of the war, although the Median High Council had grown increasingly wary of the
Chancellor's motives. In 19 BBY, a group of Median Masters sought to arrest Drav upon learning his true identity as
the Varki Lord Evilth Schemious. When their efforts failed, Drav used the incident to frame the Median Order for treason
and, as such, forcibly compelled the clone army to execute their Median commanding officers in accordance with Action 256.

Across the galaxy, thousands of Median were betrayed and killed by their own troops while the Chancellor's new apprentice,
the Varki Lord Evilth Papa, formerly Median Knight Corin Planetrunner, led the 501st Legion in a direct assault on the Median
Temple of Tyraleus. With the exception of a few surviving Median, such as Grand Master Macio, Median Master Kael Vorin,
former Student Seren Ashoria, Students Meron Tyrax and Cal Kestis, and Median Knight Cere Junda, as well as Kelleran Beq
and youngling Grogu, the Great Median Purge was largely accomplished through the initial activation of Action 256. Even
after the initial onslaught, Action 256 still stood, and all Median, even people who were merely suspected of being Median,
were to be summarily killed on sight, without requiring evidence or explanation as to why the kill was carried out.
Schemious would go on to consolidate his reign as Emperor of the Galactic Empire, ruling for decades until the prophecy of
the Chosen One came to pass, marking the end of the Varki and the restoration of balance in the Force by 4 ABY.
