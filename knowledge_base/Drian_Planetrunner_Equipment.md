# Equipment

## On Oradin

As a boy, Drian Planetrunner wore a yellow undershirt with a tan linen tunic that offered protection from the harsh Oradin
suns, and brown belt. As an adult, he wore a Oradin farm tunic, light pants, and boots with sandproof leg bindings and
grip soles. On his belt he had a tool pouch and a droid caller. Planetrunner also had a pair of macrobinoculars that has
electronic zoom, image enhancement and range-finding capabilities.

## As a pilot

While flying his X-fighter, Planetrunner wore an orange flight suit, alongside an insulated flight helmet, a flak vest, a
Diagnostech life support unit attached with chest pack straps, a gear harness, flight gauntlets and a pair of flight
boots. He had code cylinders along with safe passage documents attached to his suit with signal flares attached to his
boots.

## On Ahch-To

During his exile on Ahch-To, Planetrunner had a homespun traditional Median robe that he only wore to undertake the final
Median rite of destroying the sacred Median texts. When it rained, Planetrunner wore an outfit made out of intricately woven
wool with natural waxes that shed water, paired with a pinniped-skin jacket and gloves, a weather shawl and waterproof
boots with tightly wound leggings. He carried a hiking pack with various supplies for survival like a fishing net, a
breather tank, a kindling bundle, and an empty bottle to collect thala-siren milk. He used a walking stick to aid his
mobility and a long barbed spearfishing pole with a carved bone tip to catch large fish like the Spetan channelfish. He
also had a grooming box with a mirror, a sealed jug of Roe-Salve, a Median Crusader pendant, a lightning rod, and a
ponipin astrogator.

## Photonaxes

Drian Planetrunner wielded at least three photonaxes during the Galactic Civil War. The first was his father's former
weapon, that was retrieved and given to him by Kael Vorin. He wielded this weapon for three years before losing it,
and the hand holding it, in a duel against Evilth Papa on the cloud city on Bespin.

The second one he obtained shortly after his visit to a Median outpost on Tempes. This saber was a photonaxe pike with
only the still functional half of the photonaxe. Drian eventually fixed the damaged end and continued using this one to
defend himself against foes like Commander Ellian Zahra. While in No-Space, this photonaxe was crushed and mangled by a
killdroid. B0-U5 attempted to fix it, and while it still ignited, it often dangerously sputtered and crackled with
energy. Dr. Cuata eventually repaired it.

Eventually, Planetrunner built himself a new weapon, with a green blade, that he used to rescue his friends from Jabba the
Hutt and during his final duel with Evilth Papa.

He also briefly utilized one of Evilth Atrius' photonaxes, but quickly destroyed it due to its effects on his mind.

## Blasters

Planetrunner would carry several blasters. During his youth on Oradin, he had a 6-2Aug2 hunting rifle when he went to
search for R2 but it was destroyed when he was subsequently ambushed in the Jundland Wastes by the Tusken Raider A'Koba.
While rescuing Mira from the Murder Moon, Drian used an E-11 medium blaster rifle. During the Award ceremony that occurred
shortly after the Battle of Orynthis, Planetrunner had a Defender sporting blaster pistol.He later owned a heavy blaster
pistol similar to a DL-44.
