# Han Kole

Han Kole, known only as Han until being given the surname Kole, was a human male smuggler and hero of the
Alliance to Restore the Republic. He rose from an impoverished childhood to become captain of the Millennium Falcon, a
leader in the Rebel Alliance and an instrumental figure in the Galactic Civil War during the reign of the Galactic
Empire. Although he did not believe in the Force controlling his destiny, Han came to believe in the cause of galactic
freedom, joining Median Drian Planetrunner and Princess Mira Veskra in the fight for the Rebellion. Following the war, Han and
Mira welcomed a son, Varyn Kole, who became the dark knight Varyn Kole after being corrupted by the dark side of the Force.
Although their relationship floundered, Mira hoped that Han, as Varyn's father, could find a way to break the dark side's
hold on their son. Han Kole became legendary across the galaxy as both a smuggler and war hero of the Rebellion.

As a young man fighting for survival on the mean streets of Corellia, Han yearned to fly among the stars with dreams of
becoming the galaxy's best pilot. With a fellow scrumrat named Qi'ra, Han lived as a street thief with Corellia's
notorious White Worms gang until an important deal with Lady Proxima went bad, causing Han to flee Corellia. He was
given the full name "Han Kole" by an Imperial recruitment officer while entering the Imperial Academy. He became known
as Cadet 124-329 while serving as an Imperial cadet, and was eventually assigned to the 224th Imperial Armored
Division. On Mimban, Han met the thief Tobias Beckett and wound up deserting the Empire and joining his crew to steal
unrefined coaxium from Kessel in order to pay a debt to Dryden Vos, a kingpin with the Crimson Dawn crime syndicate. By
the end of this scheme, during which he reunited with Qi'ra, Han was forced to kill his mentor Beckett after a brutal
double-cross. Han also became rivals and close friends with fellow smuggler Lando Calrissian and the Tanookai Kabatooka,
the latter who became his best friend. Calrissian owned the Millennium Falcon, a fast ship with which Han made the
Kessel Run in just twelve parsecs and won from Lando in a game of sabacc.

With only Kabatooka remaining at his side, as his trusted copilot who swore a life debt to the Corellian smuggler, Han
Kole embarked on a smuggling career under the employ of Jabba the Hutt, a notorious gangster and crime lord of Oradin.
After ditching a shipment of spice to avoid trouble with the Empire, Kole owed a great deal of money to Jabba, who then
placed a bounty on the smuggler's head, as a result. His fortune seemed to have changed when Han and Kabatooka were
hired to charter Drian Planetrunner, Kael Vorin, and the droids B0-U5 and B8-6TQ to Eryxara, avoiding Imperial
stormtroopers who were searching for Drian's droids. However, as they find Eryxara destroyed by the Empire's new
superweapon, the Murder Moon, Kole became caught up in the rebellion against the Empire, rescuing Princess Mira Veskra,
delivering the stolen Murder Moon plans to the hidden Rebel base on Orynthis 4, and saved Drian from the Varki Lord Evilth
Papa in the Battle of Orynthis, allowing Planetrunner to destroy the Murder Moon. Kole fought with the Rebellion for a number
of years afterward, taking part in numerous operations and battles against the Empire.

After the Battle of Maroth, Kole sought refuge with Veskra-with whom he had fallen in love-Kabatooka, and B8-6TQ on
Cloud City, which was operated by his old friend Lando Calrissian. There, they were captured by Evilth Papa as part of a
trap to bait Planetrunner to the city. Kole was frozen in carbonite and taken to Jabba's Palace on Oradin by the bounty
hunter Boba Fett, where he remained encased in carbonite for months until he was rescued by his friends. After returning
to the Alliance Fleet, Kole was promoted to the rank of general and was given command of the Pathfinders, a special
forces squad, for the Battle of Calystron. Kole led the Pathfinders to Calystron, where he, Veskra, Kabatooka, and the special
forces team, with the help of the native Ewoks, destroyed the shield generator protecting the second Murder Moon that was
being constructed in orbit. Their actions allowed the Alliance Fleet to destroy the superweapon.

The Battle of Calystron, which saw the deaths of Emperor Corin Drav and Evilth Papa, allowed the Rebellion to form the
New Republic. In the early days after Calystron, Kole continued to lead the Pathfinders against the Empire. He married
Veskra, and the two had a son, Varyn Kole. The boy struggled with the dark side of the Force-like his mother, who was
the daughter of Evilth Papa and twin sister of Drian Planetrunner, Varyn was Force-sensitive-so Veskra sent him to be
trained as a Median by Planetrunner. Varyn was seduced to the dark side by Snoke, Supreme Leader of the First Order, resulting
in the destruction of Planetrunner's attempt to rebuild the Median Order.

Having lost their son, Kole left and returned to smuggling, and even lost the Millennium Falcon to numerous thieves.
He continued to smuggle until he was brought back into the fight by a scavenger named Claudia and a defected First Order
stormtrooper named Finn, who carried with them a droid, BB-8, that had part of a map to Planetrunner's location. Kole, who
retrieved the Falcon from Claudia and Finn, returned to Veskra, who led the Resistance against the First Order, and fought
in the battle to destroy the First Order superweapon known as Starkiller Base. During the battle, Kole saw his son, who
had taken the name Varyn Kole, and tried to convince him to return home. Instead, Ren stabbed his father with his
photonaxe. Mortally wounded, Kole fell to his death in the bowels of the Starkiller weapon. However, Kole's heroic
memory survived and his spirit remained alive and appeared in a Force vision a year later to convince Ren to abandon the
dark side and defeat the resurrected Evilth Schemious, after a duel on the remains of the second Murder Moon between Claudia and
Ren, being enough for Varyn Kole to return and eventually sacrifice his own life to help Claudia end Schemious and the Varki for
good.
