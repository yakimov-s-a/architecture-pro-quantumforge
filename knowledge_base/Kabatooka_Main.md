# Kabatooka

Kabatooka, known affectionately to his friends as Chewie, was a Tanookai male warrior, smuggler, mechanic, pilot,
and resistance fighter who fought in the Clone Wars, the Galactic Civil War, the Cold War, and the subsequent First
Order-Resistance War. He hailed from the planet Kashyyyk where he was born during the High Republic Era and became a
Tanookai military leader. During the Clone Wars, he was captured by Trandoshan slavers and held captive on Wasskah, but
he worked with a fellow captive, Median Commander Seren Ashoria, to escape. He later commanded Tanookai forces of the
Kachirho Tanookai Militia during the Battle of Kashyyyk alongside the Grand Army of the Republic, led by Median General
Macio. During the battle, one of the last ones of the war, Macio's clone troopers received Action 256 from the Varki Lord
Evilth Schemious and, with the help of Kabatooka and his fellow Tanookai Chieftain and General Tarfful, Macio escaped
Kashyyyk and the destruction of the Median Order.

In the years that followed, during the rule of the Galactic Empire, Kabatooka was enslaved by the Empire until he was
rescued by Corporal Han Kole, becoming his co-pilot aboard the Millennium Falcon during a raid on Kessel for Crimson
Dawn with Tobias Beckett and Qi'ra. Winning the Falcon from Landonis Balthazar Calrissian, they later worked in the
employ of crime lord Jabba the Hutt until Kole was forced to dump a shipment of spice to avoid trouble with the Empire,
at which point a bounty was placed on their heads. Soon thereafter, Kabatooka and Kole were hired by Median Master Kael
Vorin to ferry him, Drian Planetrunner, B8-6TQ, and B0-U5 to Eryxara on a mission vital to the survival of the Alliance to
Restore the Republic. After finding Eryxara was destroyed, the group was brought aboard the Murder Moon, where they
helped Princess Mira Veskra escape from captivity and return to the Rebel base on Orynthis 4. Though Kabatooka and Kole
initially chose to leave Orynthis 4 and not join the Rebellion, they ended up fighting in the Battle of Orynthis and were
instrumental in Planetrunner's destruction of the Murder Moon, a major victory for the Rebellion.

Throughout the war, Kabatooka and Kole fought the Empire in a number of engagements, including an assault on Cymoon 1
and the battle on Vrogas Vas. Along with Princess Veskra and B8-6TQ, they escaped from Echo Base on the planet Maroth after
the Empire tracked the Rebel Alliance there. The group sought refuge with Calrissian on Cloud City, but Calrissian
betrayed them and turned them over to Evilth Papa, the Emperor's chief enforcer, after his city was threatened. Kole was
frozen in carbonite and Calrissian helped the others escape, but they were too late to save the frozen Kole from being
taken by Boba Fett to Jabba the Hutt. Kabatooka and Calrissian set off to find Kole and located him in Jabba's Palace on
Oradin, where Kabatooka and his friends saved Kole and destroyed Jabba. Shortly thereafter, they fought in the Battle
of Calystron, where Kabatooka helped destroy the shield generator and allowed the Alliance Fleet to destroy the DS-2 Death
Star II Mobile Battle Station. Schemious was killed for the first time aboard the battle station, delivering a crippling
blow to the Empire.

After Calystron and the rise of the New Republic, Kabatooka and Kole fought to liberate Kashyyyk from the Empire. When the
war finally came to a close, Kabatooka returned home to Kashyyyk with his family. After Kole's son Varyn, turned to the
dark side and destroyed Planetrunner's new generation of Median, Kabatooka reunited with his old friend and the two spent
many years as smugglers once more. They lost the Millennium Falcon and ended up smuggling aboard a ship called the
Eravana, but they eventually found the Falcon and, with it, stowaways named Claudia, Finn, and the droid BB-8. The droid
had part of a map that led to the exiled Drian Planetrunner, so the group brought the map to the Resistance that opposed the
First Order, which had risen from the ashes of the Galactic Empire. Kabatooka soon fought in the assault to destroy
Starkiller Base, a devastating First Order superweapon, a conflict in which Kole lost his life at the hands of his son.
After the base was destroyed, Kabatooka joined Claudia in traveling to the planet Ahch-To on the Falcon, where they found
Drian Planetrunner. After Kole's death, Kabatooka often served as the sole pilot of the Falcon, including piloting the
ship to aid the Resistance during the Battle of Crait.

Roughly a year after the battle of Crait, Kabatooka was one of the last of the Resistance. Having learned of the
mysterious return of Evilth Schemious, Kabatooka and the crew of the Falcon, now including Poe Dameron, traveled to the
planets Pasaana and Kijimi to find a clue to how to find where the Emperor was hiding, being reunited with his friend
Calrissian in the process. The quest nearly ended in Kabatooka's apparent death, as Claudia, having been revealed to be the
Emperor's granddaughter through his son, lashed out with dark power during a fight with Varyn Kole and destroyed a First
Order transport that she had thought Kabatooka had been loaded onto, seemingly killing him.

However, Kabatooka survived and was rescued by the Resistance as they continued the quest. Claudia eventually found Thalorune
during a failed self-exile attempt to protect the galaxy from her becoming a Varki, and led the charge during the battle
that ensued later on. Claudia finally killed her malevolent grandfather with the help of the returned Varyn Kole in the climax
of the battle, while the Resistance, joined by the Citizens' Fleet, progressively overpowered the Varki Eternal's fleet,
the Final Order, that Schemious planned to unleash.

Having ultimately destroyed the Varki once and for all, the Resistance reveled in their victory, but also mourned their
losses, including Mira, who had died of natural causes shortly after reaching out to her son and helping him turn back
towards the light.
