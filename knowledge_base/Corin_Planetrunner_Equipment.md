# Equipment

## Clothing

As a slave on Oradin, Planetrunner wore a tan tunic. Equipped with tools, he was able to rebuild B8-6TQ and complete his
own podracer. During the Battle of Nivellon, he piloted a N-1 spacebattler and wore a Nivellon pilot's helmet. After becoming a
part of the Median Order, he wore Median robes and was given a Student braid. He also kept a bit driver on himself, which he
lost during the rescue of Macio, and had his own photonaxe that was destroyed on Geonosis. Afterward, he was given a
replacement saber that had a green blade for the arena battle before having built a new photonaxe for the Clone Wars.
During the early days of the war, Planetrunner wore pieces of dark colored commander armor over his Median clothing;
comprising of a gray gorget and clam shell shaped shoulder pads over maroon and blue robes. Later, he wore black and
brown Median robes much like the robes he wore prior to the Clone Wars, although he had donned the armor and maroon and
blue robes again when he met Commander Thrawn. Whilst undercover on the Halcyon, Planetrunner wore a brown and grey
outfit, which defined his muscular build, that included a cape.

During the Clone Wars, Planetrunner flew his own Delta-7B Aethersprite-class light interceptor until, later in the
conflict, he replaced it with the new Eta-2 Actis-class light interceptor. Both spacebattlers were yellow; at least in
the case of the Eta-2, the color was a tribute to his old podracer. Planetrunner also kept the Twilight, the freighter
stolen from Teth that allowed him to go on undercover missions. From the Venator-class Space Eater Resolute, the
Median General commanded a fleet of starships. As a Median General, he could also he transported to battlefields aboard a
Low Altitude Assault Transport/infantry gunship. While trying to reach the Republic Center for Military Operations,
Planetrunner and Captain Rex flew in a Praxis Mk. I turbo speeder.

During his earliest days as a Varki Lord, Evilth Papa continued to wear his Median robes and flew a green Eta-2 Actis
-class light interceptor. After burning on Dravion Prime, he was given his black life support armor that was also equipped
with a black cape. After killing Kirak Infil'a and stealing his photonaxe, Papa bled the kyber crystal to make his own
photonaxe, which he further modified after a failed assassination attempt. As a Varki, Papa flew a personal starship
during the early days of the Empire, black Eta-2 fighters, and the REU Advanced x1. Drav also gave Papa the old
Nivellon Royal Starship of Dan Liora. After being tortured by Schemious and left on Dravion Prime, Papa repaired his armor
using parts from the remains of battle droids and restored his green Eta-2. He also obtain an ancient wayfinder. After
again saying he would serve his Varki Master, his armor was repaired.

## Photonaxes

Corin Planetrunner wielded at least four photonaxes throughout his life. The first was a photonaxe constructed and used
by Planetrunner as a Student learner and Kael Vorin's apprentice. When his photonaxe was destroyed on a droid factory
assembly line on Geonosis, Planetrunner briefly used a fallen Median's green-bladed weapon during the Battle of Geonosis and
a photonaxe duel with Count Rooka, also the Varki Lord Evilth Destroyer. Planetrunner soon constructed a second blue-bladed
weapon that was similar to his first, which he used throughout the Clone Wars, in which he battled a variety of
opponents, from Rooka to his Varki apprentice Asajj Ventress, to bounty hunters such as Cad Bane. Even after Corin
Planetrunner turned to the dark side and became the Varki Lord Evilth Papa, he continued to use his Median weapon when he was
ordered to attack the Median Temple on Tyraleus and kill the Separatists on Dravion Prime. Following a duel with Vorin on
Dravion Prime, the former Median's master took the weapon after the now-Varki Lord's defeat; Vorin eventually gave the weapon
to Corin's son, Drian. Papa constructed a new weapon, one that was a black-alloy version of his original weapon with a
red blade, that he used throughout the Imperial Era. The Varki Lord used his photonaxe against surviving Median, including
his former Median apprentice Seren Ashoria and several rematches with his old master Vorin. Although Papa's photonaxe won
in battle against Drian's use of Corin's photonaxe, the Varki's photonaxe would be lost in his final duel with Drian and
his death.
