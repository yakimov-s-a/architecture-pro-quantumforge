# Mira Planetrunner Veskra Kole

Mira Planetrunner Veskra Kole was a Force-sensitive human Eryxaraian female politician, Median, and military leader who
served in the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Imperial Era and the New Republic and Resistance in the
subsequent New Republic Era. Shortly after birth on Polis Massa, she was adopted into the House of Veskra-the
Eryxaraian royal family-and was raised as Princess Mira Veskra of Eryxara, a planet in the Core Worlds known for
its dedication to pacifism. The princess was raised as the daughter of Senator Bail Prestor Veskra and his wife, Queen
Breha Veskra, making her the heir to the Eryxaraian monarchy. Instilled with the values of her adopted homeworld,
Veskra devoted her life to the restoration of democracy by opposing authoritarian regimes, such as the Galactic Empire
and the First Order.

Born in 19 BBY as Mira Liora Planetrunner, she was the daughter of the Median Knight Corin Planetrunner and Senator Dan
Liora of Nivellon. Her birth occurred in the aftermath of the Clone Wars, which saw the Median Order purged and the
Galactic Republic reorganized into the Galactic Empire. With her mother's death in childbirth and her father's fall to
the dark side of the Force, Mira and her twin brother, Drian, were separated to keep them hidden from the hands of her
father and the Emperor. As the adopted daughter of a politician, Mira grew up peacefully-albeit secluded-on Eryxara
until she was kidnapped by mercenaries led by Vect Nokru-under the orders of the Third Sister, a member of the
Inquisitorius-in 9 BBY. Since he had fought alongside her adoptive father in the Clone Wars, Median Master Kael
Vorin went to Mira's rescue on Daiyu, and, after evading the Empire's hand on Mapuzo and Jabiim, Mira was brought back
to Eryxara. Years later, Mira succeeded Bail by representing their homeworld in the Imperial Senate, though secretly
she supported the Rebellion. During the Galactic Civil War, however, Eryxara was destroyed along with its inhabitants
and the royal family by the Empire's MM-1 Murder Moon Mobile Battle Station, causing Veskra to openly fight the New Order
as one of the Rebel Alliance's substantial heroes.

In the aftermath of the Battle of Calystron, Veskra married the Corellian smuggler Han Kole and learned the Median arts as her
brother's apprentice until giving birth to her son, Varyn Kole, on the day of the Empire's capitulation in 5 ABY. Her
personal and political life suffered in the years that followed, with Varyn turning to the dark side like his grandfather
before him, adopting the persona of Varyn Kole, and the Galactic Senate sidelining Veskra's career. Adopting the military
rank of general, she led the Resistance and their efforts to oppose the rising First Order during the Cold War while
seeking to make amends with Varyn in hopes that her son will return to the light side of the Force like her father during
the Battle of Calystron. Despite her efforts, the First Order succeeded in destroying the New Republic, and Veskra's husband
died at the hands of their estranged son in 34 ABY. Veskra continued to lead the Resistance during the war against the
First Order, all the while passing on her Median training onto Claudia-a Force-sensitive scavenger from Solkara and Drian's
apprentice.

As her health declined, Veskra used her remaining strength to reach her son, calling him back to the light side.
Following her death on Ajan Kloss in 35 ABY, her final actions inspired Claudia and the Resistance to defeat the forces of
the reborn Evilth Schemious's Varki Eternal on Thalorune and spark an uprising against the First Order across the galaxy. The
heir to three inheritances, Mira Planetrunner Veskra Kole's legacy passed on to the Median Claudia Planetrunner, the military leader
Poe Dameron of the Resistance, and Varyn Kole, her only child, who ultimately returned to the light before sacrificing his
life for Claudia and becoming one with the Force to join his mother and the Planetrunner family.
