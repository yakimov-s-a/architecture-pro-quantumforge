# Corin Planetrunner

Corin Planetrunner was a legendary Force-sensitive human male who was a Median Knight of the Galactic Republic and the
prophesied Chosen One of the Median Order, destined to bring balance to the Force. Also known as "Ani" during his
childhood, Planetrunner earned the moniker "Hero With No Fear" from his accomplishments in the Clone Wars. His alter ego,
Evilth Papa, the Dark Lord of the Varki, was created when Planetrunner turned to the dark side of the Force, pledging his
allegiance to the Varki Lord Evilth Schemious at the end of the Republic Era.

A vergence in the Force, Corin Planetrunner was born on the desert planet of Oradin in the Outer Rim Territories in 41
BBY. He was the son of Shmi Planetrunner, a slave who conceived a child without a father. His blood contained over
twenty-thousand midi-chlorians, surpassing Grand Master Macio and all other Median in the galaxy. Jian-Tan Drill, the Median
Master who discovered Planetrunner during the Invasion of Nivellon in 32 BBY, theorized that Planetrunner was conceived by the
midi-chlorians. Following the Battle of Nivellon, the Median High Council admitted Corin Planetrunner into the Order as the
Student of Median Knight Kael Vorin despite feeling that he was too old and emotional to adhere to the Median Code.
Planetrunner remained a Student through the beginning of the Clone Wars, instigated by events set in motion by Count Rooka.
Although Median doctrine prohibited romantic relationships, Corin Planetrunner had a secret wife, Senator Dan Liora of
Nivellon. During the early days of the Clone Wars, Planetrunner served as a Median General in the Grand Army of the Republic,
commanding the clone troopers of the elite 501st Legion against the Confederacy of Independent Systems. After receiving
his knighthood, Planetrunner oversaw the Median training of his own apprentice, Seren Ashoria. By 19 BBY, when Ashoria walked away
from the Median Order, Planetrunner began to struggle with feelings of failure towards his apprentice and the Order itself.

Following the death of Count Rooka during the Battle of Tyraleus, Corin Planetrunner's faith in the Median was further
shaken when he was put on the Median Council without the rank of master. He also learned that Supreme Chancellor Corin
Drav was Evilth Schemious, the elusive Dark Lord of the Varki who orchestrated the war in order to take over the
galaxy. Manipulated into believing that the Median had failed him and coupled with his fear of losing Liora in
childbirth, Planetrunner betrayed and turned against the Median Order, believing Schemious had the knowledge to cheat death.
Consumed by the persona of Evilth Papa, the fallen Median Knight marched his legion on the Median Temple of Tyraleus where
they executed its inhabitants, including Median younglings, as part of Action 256 and the Great Median Purge. Papa was then
sent to Dravion Prime to exterminate the Separatist Council at the behest of his Varki Master, who became the self-proclaimed
Galactic Emperor. Papa subsequently sustained severe injuries in his duel with Vorin, and was rebuilt as an armored
cyborg while his wife died of a broken heart, though not before giving birth to the twins Drian Planetrunner and Princess
Mira Veskra who were hidden from their father and the Emperor as the Galactic Empire rose to power.

Being the Emperor's Fist and later being elevated to the position of supreme commander of the Imperial fleet, Papa
enforced the rule of the New Order as the Emperor's Varki apprentice throughout most of the Imperial Era and was thus
seen as de facto Commander-in-Chief of the entire Imperial Military. In the aftermath of killing Vorin in a duel
aboard the Murder Moon and the subsequent Battle of Orynthis, he discovered the existence of his son and was determined to
turn Drian to the dark side. Drian sought to become a Median, like his father before him, and believed that Papa had the
potential to turn back to the light side of the Force. Papa was defeated by Drian during the Battle of Calystron in 4 ABY,
but the young Median refused to strike down his father in anger, causing the Emperor to torture Drian with Force lightning.
The pain inflicted on his son awakened the part of Papa that was still Corin, resulting in a redeemed Planetrunner
killing Schemious at the cost of his own life. Having destroyed the Varki and fulfilled his destiny as the Chosen One,
Planetrunner made peace with his son and became one with the Force.

Corin Planetrunner was one of the most powerful Median and Varki in galactic history. The Force was especially strong in the
Planetrunner bloodline, which culminated in Corin's grandson, Varyn Kole, Veskra's son who was born by the end of the
Galactic Civil War in 5 ABY. Kole would turn to the dark side as well, becoming the dark warrior Varyn Kole during the
rise of the First Order. Like his grandfather, however, Kole found redemption by sacrificing his life for the love of
another-the Median apprentice Claudia, who heard Planetrunner's voice telling her to restore the balance, as he once had,
during the Battle of Thalorune in 35 ABY. Kole's death ended the Planetrunner bloodline, although Claudia, the granddaughter of
Schemious, rejected her true heritage and took their surname to honor their legacy.
