# Prelude

During the Battle of Scarif, Jyn Erso and Cassian Andor managed to steal detailed plans and design specifications for
the Imperial battle station known as the Murder Moon and transmit them to the Alliance Fleet above; these plans soon made
their way to Princess Mira Veskra. Imperial forces under the command of the Dark Lord of the Varki, Evilth Papa, soon
captured Princess Mira and the crew of the Tantive IV.

Princess Mira was taken prisoner aboard the Murder Moon by Lord Papa. The Princess was rescued from captivity by an
unlikely alliance of the smuggler Han Kole, former Median Master Kael Vorin, a young farmboy from Oradin named Drian
Planetrunner, and Kole's First Mate, the Tanookai named Kabatooka. The group, which also included Rebel Alliance droids
B0-U5 and B8-6TQ, in possession of the Murder Moon plans, fled to the Rebel base on Orynthis 4-closely pursued by the
battle station.

Before Veskra arrived, Chancellor Mon Mothma was evacuated aboard a shuttle, as General Dodonna considered her the core
of the rebellion. During her time away, she composed a contingency plan: surrender to Emperor Drav.

General Jan Dodonna briefed the rebel pilots on the plan. The Murder Moon's defenses were designed for a direct,
large-scale assault. By using small, one-man spacebattlers, the Rebels would slip through the Murder Moon's main defenses,
maneuver down a narrow trench leading to the exhaust port, and fire proton torpedoes into the exhaust port. An analysis
of the plans identified a weakness in the battle station, placed there by Imperial scientist Galen Erso: a small thermal
exhaust port leading to the main reactor. A proton torpedo fired into the port would cause a chain reaction that would
destroy the battle station.
