# Powers and abilities

## Photonaxe abilities

Despite being naturally extremely talented, Corin Planetrunner was initially lax with his photonaxe training, though he
believed that he could rival the Median Masters. Despite his relative laziness, Planetrunner was the best duelist in his
class, even though he started later than his classmates. In his training he preferred photonaxe combat over Force
mastery, and he spent more time on the physical aspects of becoming a Median. By the time he was nineteen, Planetrunner was
so gifted and skilled in dueling that he believed he already rivaled Master Macio. When the Clone Wars began, Planetrunner
was a skilled enough duelist to contend against Count Rooka, who was one of the most skilled swordsmen in the galaxy.
During the war, his frequent engagements in duels with individuals such as the informal Varki apprentice Asajj Ventress,
Savage Opress, Count Rooka, and fallen Median Barriss Offee allowed him to greatly improve his capabilities with a
photonaxe. By the end of the war, Planetrunner had the skill to single-handedly defeat Rooka in combat, despite the fact
that the Count was one of very few duelists who could hold his own against Macio.

During his duel with Vorin after his fall to the dark side, Planetrunner proved to be equally matched with his former
master, and was only defeated when Vorin exploited his hubris and blinding rage to dismember him. Planetrunner was
considered by some amongst the Median, such as Meron Tyrax, to have been the greatest warrior that the Order had during
the Clone Wars, with the only possible exceptions being Macio and Mace Windu. His former Student, Seren Ashoria, once
acknowledged Planetrunner as someone powerful who rarely lost his battles.

Planetrunner was particularly skilled in Form V-both the Shien and Djem So variants. He was able to deflect not only
blaster fire with ease, but also shots from freighters. He made good use of Djem So's strength-based strikes and
counterattacks to overpower or overwhelm his opponent. Planetrunner also used Jar'Kai on some occasions: He used Vorin's
photonaxe during his first duel with Count Rooka on Geonosis, he wielded Barriss Offee's own photonaxe against her
along with his own, and he wielded Rooka's photonaxe in addition to his own to execute him after defeating him on the
Invisible Hand.

Planetrunner was also adept in the use of Form IV, often incorporating its techniques (primarily acrobatics) into his
dueling style. He was well aware that the key to deflecting the fire of multiple adversaries was fluidity of motion,
which he mastered enough to having adjusted the classic techniques of Form IV to better defend against ranged attackers.
He demonstrated this in a holographic recording, later found in Meron Tyrax' holocron.

After Evilth Papa received his cybernetic enhancements and armor, his dueling style became visually more direct than it
had been, presumably due to his armor and cybernetics affecting his physical abilities; however, Papa's style was still
similar enough to how he fought as a Median that Admiral Tarkin was able to puzzle out Papa's former identity largely by
observing him fight. Papa continued to use Jar'Kai on various occasions. On Dravion Prime, he Force pulled a smuggler's
photonaxe and wielded it with his own to finish off the last remaining droid in Lady Corvax's sanctum. Another time,
Papa used his weapon with one of Evilth Atrius's photonaxes against a group of thugs whom Sana Starros had hired to
keep the Imperials off her back.

A pragmatic combatant both as Planetrunner and later as Evilth Papa, he had the skill to throw an activated photonaxe to
attack, guiding it with the Force, or he would resort to lethally activating unsuspecting opponents' photonaxes. He
once impressed Vorin by telekinetically activating photonaxes (both in the arms dealer Chong's possession) and cutting
him apart before pulling the photonaxes to his hands.

## Force powers

Blessed with impressive reflexes that were typical of a Median, Median Master Jian-Tan Drill saw enormous potential in the
9-year-old Planetrunner and believed him to have been conceived by the Force itself. Indeed, when his blood was tested,
Planetrunner's midi-chlorian count was off the scale-above 20,000, reportedly even greater than Macio's. When he was nine,
Corin was already skilled in having quick reflexes and precognition. This was something he used to an advantage when he
participated in pod racing. He was also able to sense what things were without looking at them. Papa was skilled at
absorbing energy with his hands, using this ability to shield himself from Han Kole's blaster fire on Cloud City and,
more surprisingly, from a weapon loaded with a kyber crystal.

In his teen years, Planetrunner's Force training progressed to the point where he was able to manifest various abilities,
and Vorin thought of him as being "advanced for his age." He was very skilled at using the Force to scan his
surroundings, such as when he sensed poisonous kouhuns infiltrating Senator Dan Liora's bedroom from standing
outside. He was also skilled at sensing other people's feelings and disturbances in the Force. Planetrunner also showed
aptitude for precognitive dreams, as when he foresaw the deaths of both his mother and his wife in dreams, but also of
himself becoming a Median, back when he lived with his mother on Oradin. However, Planetrunner struggled in using the Force
to control and befriend animals, noting that Vorin was much better at it than he was.

Planetrunner was skilled in telekinesis. Even as a late beginner, by his own admission, he was very good at it, better than
any of his peers and most of the older Students as well. He could lift photonaxes in the air and ignite them with ease,
as he did once to scare a pair of Students who had called him a "slave to his emotions." Despite having an exceptionally
strong connection to the Force, Planetrunner tended not to use it very often during photonaxe duels and focused instead on
the physical aspects of combat. This tendency held even when facing opponents who made liberal use of the Force during
duels, such as Count Rooka. He also could make use of animal control.

During the Clone Wars, Corin's power and skill continued to increase. He became capable of utilizing telekinesis to
accomplish such esoteric tasks as creating air bubbles around another person's head to enable breathing underwater, as
shown when he saved Dan's life at Mon Cala. During his mission to Utapau, Corin was able to feel brain waves on a
dead body, and when he first met Grand Admiral Thrawn, he was able to feel his thoughts by reaching out to him through
the Force. At one point during the war, Planetrunner was able to telekinetically subdue both the Son and the Daughter
simultaneously. However, this empowerment was lost when the Dark side grew too powerful and disrupted the balance in the
Force.

After his cybernetic reconstruction, Papa's connection to the Force strengthened considerably, to the point where his
master Evilth Schemious once complimented his powers as being "unparalleled." Though his injuries had physically crippled
him, Papa believed that the suffering that he had experienced had both perfected his spirit and freed him from the
needs of the flesh, allowing him to concentrate solely on his relationship to the Force. He demonstrated enough
telekinetic power to stop an AT-AT from stepping on him and had been in the process of tearing it apart before he was
interrupted in doing so. On another occasion he telekinetically lifted two AT-DP walkers off of himself without any
apparent strain. He also demonstrated the ability to grab hold of an engine-damaged Twi'lek freighter and bring it to
the ground, and crush a Lambda-class shuttle's engines in midflight to take it down. He was able to utilize a Force
push in a wave-like manner that was powerful enough to blow apart numerous lyleks simultaneously, despite the fact that
the creatures' carapaces were impervious to even heavy blaster fire.

Papa could also project Force barriers to protect himself and others. These barriers were powerful enough to protect
him while submerged in the lava of Dravion Prime, though only for a short time. Papa was able to create a barrier strong
enough to protect his son, Drian Planetrunner, from an attack by Sly Moore, who used two kyber crystal powered weapons at
the same time. On at least one occasion, he demonstrated that he was able to telekinetically manipulate targets without
the need to gesture at them as virtually all other Force adepts did. This granted him the ability to throw objects with
the Force whilst actively engaged in swordplay. He was also capable of using the Force Stasis ability and was skilled
enough in its execution that on one occasion he used it to freeze two Inquisitors simultaneously.

Papa was also capable of using his telekinetic abilities to perform extremely delicate, complex, and precise tasks. On
one occasion, he returned his badly damaged cybernetic limbs to perfect working order by dismantling a broken droid and
telekinetically repairing himself with its components. He also demonstrated the ability to crush internal organs with
the Force. At one point during an interrogation he used the Force to inflict torturous amounts of pain on several
insurgent prisoners, to the point of causing heart failure in one of them.

Planetrunner developed a tendency to use Force choke over the course of his time as a Median, especially when angered or
using it as a means of interrogation when an individual was uncooperative. Planetrunner would go on to use this power to
deadly effect when he assumed the name Papa, generally using the choke to kill those who insulted him or failed to meet
his expectations as well to instill fear. He also used the technique to twist, crush, or snap his victim's neck, notably
executing Commander Fox this way as well as other clone troopers and later stormtroopers.

Like most Force adepts, Papa also had the ability to vastly augment both his strength and speed with the Force. He was
also very skilled at using the Force to leap great distances. During the mission to investigate Geonosis, Captain Rex
remarked that Median Knight Meron Tyrax had yet to demonstrate Planetrunner's skill with Force jumps, despite having just
jumped across a chasm more than 40 meters wide.

Papa demonstrated on numerous occasions the ability to probe the thoughts of others, including those of Force adepts of
similar power to himself. Due to his strong connection with his son, Drian, he was able to sense his presence through the
Force quite easily-as opposed to his master, who was skeptical about this ability, and quickly became able to mentally
communicate with him upon Drian learning he was Papa's son.

## Other skills

Even at a young age, Planetrunner demonstrated a great aptitude for piloting, mechanics, and engineering. He rebuilt the
protocol droid B8-6TQ, an advanced droid fluent in over six million forms of communication, as well as a functional
podracer. Although during his adulthood, he would rarely use his talent as a mechanic, he kept the skill of repairing
technology. Papa had displayed great power in hunting and exterminating the Median Order, leaving his later efforts
against enemies like Crimson Dawn to require far less effort. Once most Median had been killed, Tarkin believed that Papa
was actively searching for stimulating opponents to challenge himself.

As a pilot, Planetrunner showed an amazing ability that few were able to surpass, and Vorin would later describe him to
his son Drian as "the best star pilot in the galaxy." Corin was able to pilot a pod racer and was the first and only
human thus far to compete in a pod race. He was also able to survive the Battle of Nivellon, and he went on to improve his
skills in flying and aerial combat. Throughout the Clone Wars, Corin was known for being the best pilot in the Republic
and was known for leading the charge in battles and coming out alive. He was very skilled in chasing down targets,
shaking off-target locks, and outmaneuvering opponents. His strength in the Force also gave him quick reflexes, an
ability that would help in dodging fire and focusing in on enemy targets. He also displayed incredible precognition, as
he was capable of predicting his targets' movements during dogfights with pinpoint precision. He once managed to land
the Invisible Hand when it was half-destroyed and breaking up in the atmosphere.

After becoming Papa, he remained an exceptionally skilled pilot, as shown when he single-handedly almost destroyed
Phoenix Squadron in a lone REU Advanced x1, easily out-flying their entire fleet and forcing their command to abandon
ship. He also obliterated several Alliance squadrons during the Battle of Vrogas Vas. Despite this, Planetrunner would
occasionally be shot down during his combat piloting career, with Seren Ashoria once proclaiming they always crashed when
he flew, although Planetrunner had a habit of blaming whatever ship it was he was flying. Although he mainly used
photonaxes, Planetrunner was extremely proficient at the use of blaster pistols. He also had some training on the use of
staves, a skill he once taught to a Felucian village so that they could defend themselves. Planetrunner spoke Galactic
Basic Standard, Huttese, Jawa Trade Language, and Meese Caulf, and he understood binary and the Bith language. As Papa,
he learned the ancient Varki tongue, and he spoke it along with his master, Schemious.
