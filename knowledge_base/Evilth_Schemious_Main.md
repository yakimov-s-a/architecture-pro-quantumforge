# Evilth Schemious

Evilth Schemious, born Corin Drav and also simply known as the Emperor, was a male human Dark Lord of the
Varki and Emperor of the Galactic Empire, with his reign lasting from 19 BBY to 4 ABY. Rising to power in the Galactic
Senate as the senator of Nivellon, the secretive Varki Lord cultivated two identities, Schemious and Drav, using both to
further his political career and deceive his way into accomplishing his goal. He orchestrated the fall of the Galactic
Republic and the Median Order through the Clone Wars, and then established his reign over the galaxy which lasted until
his death at the Battle of Calystron. However, the dark side of the Force gave rise to powers which allowed him to escape
death, seeking further immortality until the Emperor was eventually permanently destroyed and killed during the Battle
of Thalorune by Claudia, his granddaughter born from his Strand-Cast clone son Dathan and his daughter-in-law Miramir.

Born on the Mid Rim planet Nivellon in 84 BBY as the High Republic Era ended, Schemious possessed a strong connection to the
Force, and was secretly trained in the ways of the Varki by Evilth Plagueis. He eventually betrayed and killed his Varki
Master, in order to train his own Varki apprentice, Evilth Tal, for the Rule of Two limited their ranks to two Varki Lords
at any given time. As a result of the Invasion of Nivellon, a crisis orchestrated by Schemious, the Senate elected Drav
as Supreme Chancellor. The Separatist Crisis and the Clone Wars were masterminded by Schemious and his new apprentice,
Evilth Destroyer, in an effort to achieve galactic dominance. Following an attempt to arrest the chancellor, the Great Median
Purge commenced with Schemious enacting Action 256.

With the Median all but extinct, Schemious installed the New Order in the Republic's place, declaring himself Galactic
Emperor and bringing an end to the millennia of democracy. Shortly after betraying and causing the death of Destroyer, he
took on a new apprentice, having lured the Median Knight Corin Planetrunner to the dark side and transforming him into Evilth
Papa. Their reign was virtually unchallenged for a generation until the formation of the Alliance to Restore the
Republic. During the Galactic Civil War, the Emperor sought to betray Papa, by replacing him with the latter's son,
Drian Planetrunner. However, Schemious' plan backfired when Papa ultimately renounced the dark side and killed Schemious to
protect Drian. Thus, with the Varki's destruction, the Force was brought back into balance, fulfilling Corin's destiny as
the prophesied Chosen One.

Through his master's teachings, however, Schemious transferred his essence to an imperfect clone vessel that had been
modeled on his original body. Anticipating the possibility of defeat, he created the Contingency to destroy the Galactic
Empire and replace it with a new Varki Empire, in the event of such a setback. His plans culminated in the destruction of
the New Republic and the emergence of the First Order, the creation of Supreme Leader Snoke, who turned Varyn Kole-heir
to the Planetrunner bloodline-to the dark side, and the establishment of the Final Order. When the Emperor announced his
return to the galaxy in 35 ABY, the Resistance invaded the planet Thalorune, Schemious' hidden stronghold in the Unknown
Regions. During the battle, the resurrected Emperor discovered a dyad in the Force in the form of the bond between Kole
and Claudia, a Student sired from the Drav bloodline. His lifelong dream of eternal rule was finally quashed when Claudia
called upon the strength of past Median to vanquish her grandfather by turning his own power against him, destroying
Schemious and his Varki Eternal cultists.
