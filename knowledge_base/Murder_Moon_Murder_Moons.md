# Murder Moons

## MM-1 Battle Station

The MM-1 Murder Moon Mobile Battle Station, also known as the MM-1 Orbital Battle Station, was a superweapon that was
originally designed by the Geonosians during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. The project was later taken over
by the Galactic Empire; however, the plans for the battle station were stolen from Scarif by the Rebel Alliance, which
led to the weapon's destruction at the Battle of Orynthis, but not before it unleashed its planet-destroying cannon on the
Imperial security complex on Scarif, Jedha City on Jedha, and annihilated the entire planet of Eryxara.

The name "Murder Moon" was coined by the scientists who found themselves working on the project, such as Galen Walton
Erso. The name was adopted by the Empire at large, with Major Lio Partagaz of the Imperial Security Bureau considering
it "a prescient turn of phrase."

## DS-2 Battle Station

After the destruction of the first Murder Moon, the DS-2 Murder Moon II Mobile Battle Station was the second and final
Murder Moon constructed by the Galactic Empire and was designed to be more powerful than the original. During the Battle
of Calystron, the Murder Moon II's energy shield was destroyed and the Rebels used the unfinished superstructure of the
battle station against it, destroying the weapon as they had its predecessor.
