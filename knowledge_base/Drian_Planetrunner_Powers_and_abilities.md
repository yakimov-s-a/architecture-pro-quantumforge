# Powers and abilities

## Photonaxe abilities

Though he received little formal training, Drian Planetrunner had a natural talent for photonaxe combat. Drian's initial
training with the weapon of the Median came during a brief session with Kael Vorin and a training remote on the way to
Eryxara, where he learned the initial position and the four defensive postures. Though his level proved inadequate
during his first, brief duel against Evilth Papa on Cymoon 1, his combat skills had improved for their duel on Bespin,
and culminated with him besting the Varki Lord in combat during the Battle of Calystron.

Like most Median, Planetrunner was adept at redirecting blaster fire, and eventually was able to do it while flipping. During
his mission to Rodia, he tried his hand at Jar'Kai for the first time with his and Huulik's photonaxe. Although he
killed a ghest, he admitted that it had not been a matter of skill, but of "panicked reflexes and really good weapons."
He received further training from the "Gamemaster," a servant of Grakkus the Hutt, who had learned the art of photonaxe
combat. Forced to fight him and the Hutt's MagnaGuards, Planetrunner improved his cursory abilities. He was able to fight
and defeat Kreel of SCAR Squadron in photonaxe combat, with help from utilizing his surroundings, and could easily take
down Imperial droids.

After a brief time of training with Macio, Planetrunner revealed himself to be a gifted duelist on Bespin. Planetrunner engaged
Papa in a fierce duel and managed to hold his own for much of the duel, wounding Papa in the shoulder, impressing the
Dark Lord with his skill, though Papa ultimately bested his son and cut off his hand, proving his superiority. After
recovering from his loss to Papa, Drian defeated the spirit of Grand Inquisitor in a duel on Tempes. The Inquisitor
insulted his photonaxe ability and suggested that he fought like a child, but Planetrunner was still able to defeat him.

In one training session aboard Home One, Drian faced off against nine training remotes simultaneously. He easily
destroyed the remotes in quick succession, finishing the last one off while in the middle of a conversation with B8-6TQ.
However, when he attempted to fight killdroids with his photonaxe while in No-Space, he first believed that the droids
were resistant to photonaxes, before realizing that they were not resistant, he just had not been focused and one with
the Force. After re-centering himself, Planetrunner was able to cut through the droids. He also successfully defeated the
droid Ajax Sigma using a photonaxe, though the droid escaped. He also fought many droids on Epikonia and was able to
take many down with his photonaxe by consistently focusing on staying calm and concentrating on the Force.

Planetrunner showed his abilities in battle during the rescue of Han Kole, where a skirmish ensued in which the Median killed
many guards aboard the execution skiff and Jabba's sail barge. Planetrunner also utilized his abilities on Calystron, blocking
laser fire from a scout trooper on a speeder bike before causing the trooper to crash after severing off the speeder's
front stabilizers. Later, on board the second Murder Moon, Planetrunner showed his improved abilities and was finally able
to duel Papa on an even footing. However, his unwillingness to fight his own father partly held him back; but when
Papa threatened to turn Mira to the dark side, an enraged Planetrunner unleashed all of his raw ability, overwhelming and
defeating the Varki Lord.

Drian was able to hack his way through the stormtroopers and hostile native creatures on Pillio, though he admitted that
he would have preferred to solve the issue without violence if he had not been forced to defend himself. During his
rescue of Grogu from Moff Gideon, Planetrunner easily destroyed a platoon of Dark Troopers with his photonaxe and the
Force. Later in life, Planetrunner taught multiple students in the art of photonaxe combat, including his nephew Varyn Kole,
who would later become his strongest student and destroy his Median Order. During Varyn's training, Planetrunner encountered
and effortlessly overpowered the Knights of Ren, seamlessly dodging their attacks and destroying their weapons before
using the Force to disarm them. When Drian dueled Exim Panshard, he discovered that his skills with a blade far surpassed
those of the ancient Varki Lord. Drian easily defeated the Varki despite restraining himself to avoid harming Panshard's
host Kiza.

When fighting an angry Claudia using her quarterstaff, Planetrunner fluidly dodged her initial assault, then using a metal rod,
blocked all her strikes before hitting her once and then disarming her. Later, on Crait, Drian proved his superiority in
skill over Varyn Kole as well by avoiding all of Ren's attacks without even needing to use his photonaxe to block,
distracting the First Order long enough for the remaining members of the Resistance to escape.

## Force powers

Like his father, Planetrunner possessed a uniquely powerful connection to the Force, and became one of the most powerful
Force wielders in galactic history. Indeed, Evilth Schemious feared Planetrunner and believed that he was strong enough in the
Force to destroy the Varki. Most of Drian's understanding of the Force came from his own research, exploration, and
experience as he only had a few days Vorin's mentorship before his demise, Macio's one year of mentorship before he died
of old age, and Vorin's sporadic guidance from beyond the Netherworld of the Force.

After learning the basics of the Force, he quickly learned how to sense through it, and took the habit of opening
himself to the Force in hopes of learning more than Vorin had told him, and becoming aware of his surroundings. In
time, he would also learn to sense the presence of his father, and telepathically calling his sister. He was capable of
seeing and hearing those who had become one with the Force. By using the Force during the Battle of Orynthis, he managed to
destroy the Murder Moon with a shot that many thought was impossible.

After obtaining Huulik's photonaxe and dismantling it to know his inner workings, Planetrunner surmised on his own that
the Force could be used to manipulate matter in the manner of telekinesis and managed to learn how to move small
objects, such as a noodle or a fork. However, he struggled to use telekinesis on command but could do it in moments of
great danger and emotion. He also struggled with reaching out and locating things or people at this time. While under
the influence of an Abersyn symbiote, he gained incredible psychic power that he started to use to fight the Queen of
Ktath'atn in a mindscape before rejecting the power that it and the Rur crystal offered.

By the time of the Battle of Maroth, Planetrunner had the ability to pull his photonaxe to his hands, though not without
difficulty. Under Macio's training, this ability was further developed, enabling him to levitate several objects at once
while meditating. Shortly after the revelation that Evilth Papa was his father, Drian Planetrunner struggled with using the
Force and could not reach out to Vorin. When consumed by anger and fear over what he had learned and lost, he lashed
out with the Force, hurtling a squadron of Tie Fighters into each other. In another outburst, he accidentally cracked
the window of a starship while desperately trying and failing to contact Macio. However, he regained his connection when
he tried to reach out for his photonaxe and had a vision of Verla and that Veskra was in trouble. He then was able to
deactivate many carbonite blocks with people inside all at once with the Force and free them. He was able to use the
Force to anticipate and avoid many booby traps set by Verla in a cave. He was also able to hold back water flooding in
to a trap from the ocean with the Force long enough to convince Verla to deactivate it. Despite his connection to the
Force still being somewhat unreliable, Papa sensed that Planetrunner's strength and connection in the Force had grown
significantly.

Planetrunner eventually learned other uses, such as mind control techniques and the mind trick. He could use the Force to
influence and control creatures and was able to use it to sense others' emotions and intentions. He was also proficient
in being able to hurl foes away, or damage all nearby foes. Drian could use the Force to make great leaps. When large
amounts of death occurred, he could also sense it as a disturbance in the Force. When visibility was limited, Planetrunner
could use the Force to help him navigate in a ship.

After the activation of the Fermata Cage, Planetrunner's abilities in the Force became more difficult, beginning as just
being unsure that he could perform a mind trick, but becoming more unwieldy when he was trapped in No-Space. When trying
to use the Force to catch Lando Calrissian when he fell, Planetrunner thought that he did not do it as quick as he normally
could have and felt distracted by ripples in the Force. Later, after pushing dangerous droids away from Kabatooka, he
unusually felt like he would not be able to do so again. During this time, he could perform a massive Force push that
acted as a wave, a feat believed he would not have been able to do without the weirdness of the Force at the time, but
one that allowed him to push all of the killdroids on the Hall out into space and later destroy large crystal guardians.
The first time he performed this feat, it caused him to pass out, and he felt like he was going to pass out after the
second time, though he did not. After he first performed it, Planetrunner struggled to just pull and object toward him with
the Force, and he felt its power go in and out like waves. Some examples of this were when he tried to calm a massive
creature on Christophsis, but suddenly felt unable to touch the Force at all, and when he could not feel the Force when
he attempted to open Sason Temple. It could also cause him to not use the Force in the way he intended, such as when he
tried to hold a Starweird still and instead impaled it with many crystals.

In the Sason Temple, Planetrunner could hear and sense the frequency produced by Kythoo's Bell and its emitter in the
Force. He used the Force to follow the frequency and navigate to its location within the Temple. He could later use the
Force to activate said emitter. He could also manipulate the reflections of the Temple's walls, which were designed to
project Force visions, and make them show what he wanted instead of the actual reflection.

Prior to the Battle of Calystron, Planetrunner once attempted to purify a red kyber crystal at the direction of Cuata and
Gretta, but he failed and merely had a vision of a Varki lord before temporarily being sent to a different dimension of
the Force and seeing Papa. He was later able to connect to a raw kyber crystal, which turned green and allowed him to
speak with Macio, but the unsteadiness of the Force at that time left him trapped in his vision until Macio helped him
realize that just like the sun did not disappear during the night, the Force would also always be there. Planetrunner took
possession of the now-green crystal and took the red crystal as well, intending to one day heal it.

Years later, during his rescue of Grogu, Planetrunner used the Force to destroy Dark Troopers by throwing them off ledges,
pushing them away, smashing crates into them/flinging debris at them, and crushing them with the Force.

Planetrunner was skilled at doing mind tricks, as well as performing Force-enhanced jumps. He also demonstrated the ability
to have visions several times. Planetrunner could disconnect himself from the Force, and he was able to renew his
connection within a short amount of time. Drian Planetrunner understood the dark side powers mind probe, Force-bond, and
Force lightning well enough to write about them. On at least one occasion, he healed the bled kyber crystal from a Varki
photonaxe. He also studied Force healing and many esoteric Force powers. Snoke considered Planetrunner to be very powerful
and formidable and believed he would have taken over the galaxy quickly with Planetrunner as his apprentice.

When using the force to restrain an archeologist possessed by a Varki spirit on Yoturba, Drian had to concentrate very
carefully to restrain his power so that he did not accidentally kill the man. Shortly after this, Planetrunner called upon
his deep reservoir of power to slow the descent of a damaged REU Defender (while he was still in space on a different
starship) that was about to crash onto the diamond surface of a moon in the Taw Provode system, saving the spacebattler
and its pilot from being completely obliterated. When facing the Knights of Ren, Planetrunner effortlessly Force-pushed the
Knights of Ren and telekinetically disarmed and destroyed their weapons.

After the fall of Varyn Kole and the destruction of his Median Temple, Planetrunner spent years himself how to close himself
off from the Force, an ability he considered much more difficult than reaching out to it in the first place. Shortly
after reopening himself to the Force, Drian detected Claudia and Ren's bond and in his anger, he Force pushed Claudia's hut into
pieces around the two and broke the connection. When defending himself against Claudia, Drian pried an antenna from the roof
of a hut and summoned it to himself in seconds, then managed to keep himself suspended off the ground after being
knocked down by Claudia. Whereas Ren noted that the effort of attempting Force projection would kill Claudia, Planetrunner was able
to use the complete version of this ability; he gave all of himself into the Force, which allowed him to interact with
others by sending a life-like image of himself across the galaxy to distract Varyn Kole and the First Order. The
projection was so powerful that B8-6TQ acknowledged his presence, and Ren was tricked into believing it was the real him.
Planetrunner maintained it for a prolonged period of time, although the strain ultimately proved to be fatal and killed the
Median Master.

Planetrunner was one of the few Median who was able to truly become one with the Force and preserve his consciousness as a
Force spirit. As a Force spirit, Planetrunner was able to physically interact with the living world, which he demonstrated
when he caught the Planetrunner photonaxe after Claudia threw it, and when he lifted his X-fighter from the oceans of Ahch-To and
repaired it so that she could travel to Thalorune.

## Other abilities

Planetrunner was acknowledged to be an expert pilot, being the person who was responsible for destroying the first Death
Star and managing to evade Evilth Papa himself (albeit with help from Han Kole). He also managed to destroy an
Interdictor vessel on his own while piloting the Desert Jewel, although he acknowledged that B0-U5 saved him when the
astromech droid made the ship jump into hyperspace in the nick of time. He was also able to pilot a Imperial
Lambda-class T-4a shuttle with relative ease, outmaneuvering an RZ-1 A-wing interceptor that was trying to get a lock on
him. In fact, Planetrunner had a talent for tinkering and a passion for speed. With no previous experience, Planetrunner was
able to pilot a podracer with incredible skill, a feat that an announcer and Papa acknowledged was impressive. He was
considered to be one of the Alliance's best pilots and gained recognition and fame amongst them for his skills because
he made the shot that destroyed the Murder Moon. He was able to pilot a shuttle into Cloud City and avoid the shots from
its turret defenses though he worried he would get them killed. He briefly bested Papa in a dogfight over Jekara,
tricking him into crashing. His piloting and targeting skills were limited by his fear occasionally, and on one instance
he missed a shot because he had a vision of Papa defeating Kole during the Battle of Orynthis.

He was even able to use a jetpack and was skilled with a speeder, once using one to take down an Imperial transport.
However, he once attempted to drive a boat and ended up capsizing it. He also could perform environmental surveys,
largely to due farming skills he gained from his time on Oradin.

He was a native speaker of Galactic Basic Standard, and although Planetrunner could not understand Shyriiwook, he had
quickly learned how to read Kabatooka's moods by his tone and expression before the Battle of Orynthis. After decades of
missions with R2, Planetrunner was reasonably fluent in droidspeak.

Planetrunner was adept in unarmed combat. He demonstrated his skill early on Cymoon 1 where he defeated a few stormtroopers
by kicking and slamming them to the ground. Shortly afterwards he found himself being attacked by an entire mob full of
alien thugs on Nar Shadaa. Planetrunner defeated numerous of the attackers unarmed before igniting his photonaxe and
startling the rest. He found himself in another similar situation on Horox III, where he defeated an entire cantina of
alien thugs with the help of Aphra and her Tanookai bounty hunter. Planetrunner knocked out the Commander Kanchar, a hulking
cybernetic enhanced man with a single punch after outsmarting him. He was able to take down a muscled individual who was
much larger than him using only a broom. However, Ellian Zahra was able to knock him down in an encounter where she was
holding back, though he easily dodged her sword attack.

He was also capable with a blaster, and could also escape ropes tied around his hands quickly, a skill Doctor Aphra
theorized he learned from Han Kole. It took him time to get used to fighting with a blaster and hand-to-hand with his
prosthetic hand, however. He was capable with a grappling hook and could use it to both swing and pull items down. He
also knew how to swim.

In his later years, Planetrunner displayed such skill that he was able to easily punch aside a Knight of Ren and disarm Claudia
of her quarterstaff before she could react.
