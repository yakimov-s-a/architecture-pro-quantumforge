# Dravion Prime

Dravion Prime was a young, small planet located in the Dravion Prime system, within the Atravis sector of the galaxy's Outer
Rim Territories. Once a garden world nourished by the Bright Star artifact, its orbit was shifted when Lady Corvax
unleashed the energies of the Bright Star in an attempt to return her husband to life. The resulting gravimetric duel
between the gas giants Jestefad and Lefrani over Dravion Prime heated the planet's core, transforming the lush world into an
imbalanced volcanic hellscape. Adapting to life on their devastated homeworld, the arthropodal Dravion Primeians evolved into
two distinct subspecies that cooperated to ensure their continued survival. Legends of Corvax's search for immortality
brought the ancient Varki to Dravion Prime, seeking the same secrets of eternal life. There, they built a temple over a locus
in the dark side of the Force, above the buried ruins of Corvax Fortress.

Acquired by the Techno Union over three hundred years before the Clone Wars, Dravion Prime's lava flows were mined for unique
and valuable minerals. It was also home to the Black Sun crime syndicate, whose headquarters was located near the mining
town that became known as Zitontown after the Falleen Ziton Moj ascended to leadership of the organization. As part of
the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars, the Techno Union on Dravion Prime produced battle droids for
the Separatist Droid Army. Evilth Schemious had a secret facility on the planet where he intended to turn "harvested"
Force-sensitive children into Force-talented spies. However, the Varki Lord had the base scuttled when Median Knight Corin
Planetrunner and his Student, Seren Ashoria, arrived to rescue the younglings.

Dravion Prime served as the final redoubt of the Separatist Council until the newly-appointed Evilth Papa, formerly Corin
Planetrunner, was sent by Schemious to slaughter the council, effectively ending the Clone Wars. The Varki Lord was confronted
by his wife, Dan Liora, and his former Median Master, Kael Vorin. The latter dueled Papa, defeating him and
leaving him to burn on a lava bank. However, Papa was saved when Schemious arrived and carried him offworld. Dravion Prime
then came under the ownership of the Galactic Empire after the Techno Union and its assets were nationalized. Schemious
had new facilities built on the planet, where more Force-sensitive infants were taken and transformed into agents of the
Inquisitorius.

During this era, Papa returned to the site of his greatest defeat and, with the help of the ancient Varki Lord Evilth
Momin, erected the massive monolith Fortress Papa to serve as his personal sanctum, built on the site of the former
Varki temple. Throughout the Age of the Empire, Papa used the planet to fulfill his master's biddings, with the fiery
world becoming the final resting place for victims of the Great Median Purge. In addition, Papa also undertook personal
business within his new realm, attempting to wield the dark powers of Dravion Prime to bring his deceased wife back from the
dead.

Following the Dark Lord of the Varki, the Empire took hold of the planet. Resource extraction efforts on Dravion Prime were
taken over by the Mining Guild, although strict Imperial travel restrictions on the system made it difficult for the
guild to turn a profit and mining operations eventually ceased entirely. Black Sun also abandoned Dravion Prime for a more
low profile world when the Empire established a garrison there. The Dravion Primeians resisted the Imperial occupation of
their homeworld and attempted to drive them from Dravion Prime by launching multiple assaults on Fortress Papa.

Although it hosted one of the earliest rebel victories of the Galactic Civil War, Dravion Prime fell into obscurity following
Papa's demise at the Battle of Calystron, though Black Sun did eventually return to the planet. After the destruction of
the Bright Star, the planet's ecosystem slowly began to heal from the devastation it had endured for centuries prior.
Many devout pilgrims journeyed to the ruins of Fortress Papa, hoping to tap into the late Varki's dark power. Decades
after Papa's death, his grandson, Varyn Kole, journeyed to the world to continue the legacy of his family's grisly past.
The Supreme Leader of the First Order slaughtered the Varki cult that defended Fortress Papa and discovered his
grandfather's Varki wayfinder, a tool that allowed him to locate the phantom emperor Schemious on Thalorune.
