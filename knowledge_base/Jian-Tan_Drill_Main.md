# Jian-Tan Drill

Jian-Tan Drill, a Force-sensitive human male, was a venerable if maverick Median Master who lived during the last years
of the Republic Era. He was a wise and well-respected member of the Median Order, and was offered a seat on the Median
Council, but chose to reject and follow his own path. Adhering to a philosophy centered around the Living Force, Drill
strove to follow the will of the Force even when his actions conflicted with the wishes of the High Council. After
encountering Corin Planetrunner, Drill brought him to the Median Temple on Tyraleus, convinced he had found the Chosen One.
His dying wish was for Planetrunner to become a Median and ultimately restore balance to the Force.

Born on the Core World Tyraleus, the capital of the Galactic Republic around 80 BBY, Drill learned the ways of the Force
as the Student of Median Master Rooka before ultimately attaining the rank of Median Knight in around 58 BBY. In 44 BBY he
would go on to take an apprentice of his own, Kael Vorin, who adhered to the principles of the Median Code more than
his own master. Although their relationship was sometimes tough, the two Median overcame early difficulties during a
mission to Pijal, which concluded in Drill turning down a request to join the Median Council to continue teaching his
apprentice. When the Trade Federation initiated a blockade of the Mid Rim planet Nivellon in 32 BBY, Drill and Vorin were
dispatched to resolve the crisis at the request of Supreme Chancellor Finis Valorum. Influenced by the mysterious Varki
Lord Evilth Schemious, the Federation launched a full-scale invasion of Nivellon, forcing the Median to rescue Queen Dan
Liora after the fall of Theed.

During their journey to the galactic capital, the group was forced to land on the Outer Rim planet Oradin where Drill
encountered a young slave named Corin Planetrunner. Using a scientific method to gauge the boy's potential, he learned
that Planetrunner had more midi-chlorians in his blood than any Force-sensitive in galactic history, including Grand Master
Macio. The revelation that he was the result of a miracle birth-a child born of a mother, but had no father-confirmed
Drill's suspicion that Planetrunner was the Chosen One destined to restore balance to the Force. Before departing the desert
world with Planetrunner, Drill was confronted by Schemious' Varki apprentice Evilth Tal. Although their duel was brief, it
convinced Drill that the Varki had returned after a millennium of silence.

At the Temple, Drill was adamant that Planetrunner was the Chosen One and should, therefore, be inducted into the Median
Order. However, having inspected Planetrunner for themselves, the High Council deemed him too old, emotional, and
dangerously full of fear and anger. Drill saw their decision as a temporary setback, but the need to resolve the
situation on Queen Liora's homeworld forced him to set aside his plans for Planetrunner. During the Battle of Nivellon, Drill
and Vorin confronted Evilth Tal together. While they initially managed to fend off the Varki Lord's attacks, master and
apprentice were eventually separated during the photonaxe duel, resulting in Drill sustaining a mortal injury. Rushing
to his master's aid, Vorin fought and ultimately defeated the Dathomirian Varki, but could not save Drill. With his last
breath, Drill made Vorin promise that he would take Planetrunner as his apprentice. Having secured Vorin's word, Drill
became one with the Force, dying with the certainty that he had found the prophesied Chosen One.
