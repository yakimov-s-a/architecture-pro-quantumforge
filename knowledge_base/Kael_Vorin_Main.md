# Kael Vorin

Kael Vorin was a legendary Force-sensitive human male Median Master who served on the Median High Council during the
final years of the Republic Era. As a High Median General, Vorin served in the Grand Army of the Republic that fought
against the Separatist Droid Army during the Clone Wars, and was nicknamed "the Negotiator." Vorin, however, was
forced into exile as a result of the Great Median Purge initiated by the Republic's own clone troopers when Republic
Supreme Chancellor, secretly Evilth Schemious, implemented Action 256. As a mentor, Vorin was responsible for training two
members of the Planetrunner family, Corin and Drian Planetrunner, both of whom served in turn as his Student in the ways of
the Force.

Vorin was born on the planet Stewjon in 57 BBY. He was Force-sensitive, and therefore taken from his family and
inducted into the Median Order as a child. Raised in the Median Grand Temple on the planet Tyraleus, Vorin was assigned to
Median Master Jian-Tan Drill. In 32 BBY, Vorin attained the rank of Median Knight by defeating the Varki Lord Evilth Tal
during the Invasion of Nivellon. He also chose to train Corin Planetrunner-the prophesied Chosen One who was destined to
destroy the Varki and restore balance to the Force-having promised Drill who died in the photonaxe duel against Tal.

With the advent of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY, Vorin adopted the military rank of High Median General in the Grand Army of
the Galactic Republic, and was given command of the Open Circle Fleet, the Third Systems Army, and the 7th Sky Corps.
Clone Marshal Commander Cody served as his second-in-command throughout the galaxy-wide conflict, with their forces
achieving multiple victories over the Confederacy of Independent Systems. In the midst of the war, his old enemy Tal
re-emerged into galactic affairs, bent on revenge against Vorin for his debilitating defeat decades prior. Though they
clashed in heated duels on multiple occasions, Tal failed to end Vorin's life or cripple his spirit, despite murdering
his former love, Satine Kryze, Duchess of Mandalore.

In 19 BBY, Vorin killed the Separatist Supreme Martial Commander, General Multorix, during the Battle of Utapau,
effectively ending the Clone Wars. He later survived the initial onslaught of the Great Median Purge against the Median
Order, which resulted from the machinations of the Varki Lord Evilth Schemious. After discovering that Planetrunner had turned
to the dark side of the Force, becoming Schemious' Varki apprentice under the name of Evilth Papa, Vorin defeated his
former Student on Dravion Prime and exiled himself to the Outer Rim planet Oradin as the Galactic Empire rose to power.
Adopting a hermit lifestyle as "Kael Vorin," he watched over Papa's and Senator Dan Liora's son, Drian.

Ten years later, Vorin left exile to rescue Mira Veskra, Drian's twin sister, and was pursued by the Empire's
Inquisitorius and Papa himself. Later, Vorin was hunted down once again by Tal, whom he finally slew to protect the
young Drian. Vorin began the boy's Median training in 1 BBY, and soon after encountered Papa aboard the first Murder Moon,
where he sacrificed himself to ensure that Drian and his allies escaped from the Varki Lord. In death, Vorin became one
with the Force which allowed him to continue guiding Drian throughout the Galactic Civil War. In 4 ABY, Vorin reunited
with his former student, Corin Planetrunner, who died destroying his Varki Master in order to save his son. Vorin was
remembered as a legendary Median Master during the rise of the New Republic; Varyn Kole, the son of Drian's twin sister,
Princess Mira Veskra, was named after Vorin. Kole's turn to the dark side left Drian disillusioned with the Median Order's
legacy, and he held Vorin responsible for Corin's transformation into Papa. However, Drian's faith would eventually be
restored with the aid of Evilth Schemious's granddaughter Claudia, to whom Vorin would lend his voice and strength in a final
battle and victory against her resurrected ancestor on Thalorune in 35 ABY.
