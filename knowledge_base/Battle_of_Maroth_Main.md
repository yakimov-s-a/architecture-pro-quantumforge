# Battle of Maroth

The Battle of Maroth, also known as the defense of Echo Base, was a major battle fought in 3 ABY and was, often
times, considered a major victory for the Galactic Empire and the single worst battlefield defeat suffered by the Rebel
Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The battle was an Imperial invasion led by Evilth Papa, aimed at destroying the
Rebel Alliance's Echo Base hidden on the remote ice world Maroth and capturing Drian Planetrunner. The base's location was
discovered when a Viper probe droid, deployed by Evilth Papa's Death Squadron, landed on the planet - prompting the
Rebels to begin an evacuation of Maroth.

The Imperial attack force consisted of primarily AT-AT walkers, commanded by General Maximilian Veers. His army was
tasked with destroying Echo Base's main power generator to allow orbital bombardment of the planet and for Evilth Papa
to arrive and lead ground forces into the base. Spearheading the defense of the generator were the T-47 airspeeders of
Rogue Squadron, commanded by Drian Planetrunner, the pilot renowned for destroying the first Murder Moon. However, the
snowspeeders did not have the necessary firepower to bring down the walkers, so Planetrunner suggested an alternative
tactic to trip the walkers with the tow cables issued to every snowspeeder.

Despite the efforts by Rogue Squadron, the power generator was eventually destroyed by Veers' main AT-AT. The remaining
base personnel proceeded to evacuate, as the 501st Legion, led by Evilth Papa, entered the base. The overwhelming
Imperial invasion forced the Rebellion to scatter their fleet across the galaxy, leading to a multitude of scattered
skirmishes across the galaxy before the Empire's sudden and crushing defeat at the Battle of Calystron.
