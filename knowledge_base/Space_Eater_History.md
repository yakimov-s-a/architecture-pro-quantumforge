# History

During the Clone Wars, the Galactic Republic Navy fielded a fleet of Venator-class Space Eaters. The Venator
-class saw action in numerous battles against the Confederacy navy including the Battle of Tyraleus. Following the
transformation of the Republic into the Galactic Empire, the Republic's Space Eaters were incorporated into the new
Imperial Navy. Aside from the Venator and Imperial-class Space Eaters, other known models in Imperial service
included the Victory and Secutor-class Space Eaters. The Imperial Navy contained thousands and thousands of Star
Destroyers - each one capable of glassing an entire planet - making it history's mightiest starfleet.

During the rebellion and the Galactic Civil War, Imperial Space Eaters took part in numerous engagements against the
various rebel forces that eventually coalesced into the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Space Eaters were deployed
during the secret mission to Oradin, Battle of Maroth, and the Battle of Calystron.

Following the events at Calystron, the Rebel Alliance's successor government, the New Republic, captured and destroyed
numerous Space Eaters in several engagements including the Battle of Theed, the Rebellion on Akiva, the Defense of
Kuat, and the Liberation of Kashyyyk. By 5 ABY, Grand Admiral Rae Sloane estimated that 75% of the Imperial Star
Destroyers before Calystron had either been destroyed, captured, or lost in "confirmable if curious ways." While trawling
through the archives at the Hall of Imperial Register on Tyraleus, Sloane discovered that the remaining 25% had
mysteriously disappeared and their purported destruction had been falsified.

Later, Sloane learned that Fleet Admiral Gallius Rax secretly commanded Imperial fleets in the Almagest, the Recluse's
Nebula, the Queluhan Nebula, the Ro-Loo Triangle, and the Inamorata. These fleets consisted of hundreds of Star
Destroyers and thousands of smaller ships. Following the Attack on Chandrila, Rax ordered the Imperial fleets to
assemble above the planet Solkara. Numerous Space Eaters, including Captain Ciena Ree's starship Inflictor, saw
action during the Battle of Solkara, the cataclysmic battle that ended the Galactic Civil War in favor of the New
Republic.

Thirty years after the Battle of Calystron, the barren surface of Solkara was still littered with the wreckage of countless
Space Eaters. These derelict starships were regularly targeted by scavengers such as Claudia. The First Order, a remnant
of the Old Empire, was also known to utilize Space Eaters as part of its fleet, such as the First Order's Resurgent
-class Space Eaters, which were inspired by the dagger-shaped design of the Old Empire's vessels. The First Order's
leadership, including its Supreme Leader, were stationed aboard the gargantuan Mega-class Star Dreadnought
Supremacy, the sole ship of its class.

When the revived Evilth Schemious and his Varki Eternal cultists emerged from Thalorune and assimilated the First Order's
military after the death of Supreme Leader Snoke, the Varki Lord unveiled his own armada, the Final Order, which was
comprised of 1,080 Xyston-class Space Eaters. The entire fleet was destroyed in the Battle of Thalorune before they
could be deployed en masse.
