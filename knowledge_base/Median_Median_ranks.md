# Median ranks

## Main ranks

Members of the Median Order held various positions within the Order at times as they studied the Force and gained
experience. These ranks included:

- Median Initiate: A young child who was Force-sensitive would be identified at birth and taken to the Median Temple on
  Tyraleus to be trained as a Median. They were to be put into classes of other Median younglings and trained together
  before being taken as an apprentice by a Median Knight. Younglings were not to be trained once they had reached a
  certain age, with the sole exception to this rule being Corin Planetrunner. Younglings would participate in the
  Gathering, which was the pilgrimage to the Ilum Temple to gain a kyber crystal for their photonaxe.
- Student: A youngling who had been chosen by a Median Knight or Median Master to train under their tutelage personally
  into becoming a fully fledged Median Knight. Their readiness was decided by the Initiate Trials. Students wore a braid.
- Median Knight: Once a Student has successfully passed the Median trials, they were be granted the rank of Median Knight
  and went out on missions of their own, no longer under the tutelage of a master and may take on a Student themselves.
- Median Master: A Median Knight was granted the rank of Median Master when they had proven themselves. A select few
  masters were offered a seat on the Median High Council when they had shown great skill, wisdom and devotion to the
  Force. Some Masters became Sage Masters or Warrior Masters.
- Grand Master: The leader of the Median High Council, the position of Grand Master was given to the oldest and
  considered to be the wisest member of the Median Order.

## Median occupations

Some members of the Median Order had specific titles and roles within the Order. These included:

- Median Advisor: A Median who worked as a personal advisor.
- Master of the Order: This position was assumed by the leader of the Median High Council. They had to be voted
  unanimously into the position by the council's members.
- Median Temple Guard: Headed by Cin Drallig during the Clone Wars, these anonymous Median guarded the Median Temple with
  photonaxe pikes.
- Median investigator: These Median were detectives who aided police with the use of the Force. They also used crime
  scene analysis droids.
- Consular Median: These Median devoted themselves to the study of diplomacy or science and avoided combat and warfare
  duties.
- Chief Librarian: This Median oversaw the Median Archives and Holocron Vault.
- Median seeker: These Median searched the galaxy for Force-sensitive children in order to recruit them into the Median
  Order.
- Median Sentinel: Median who focused on small-scale, community matters.
- Median Guardian: Median also known as "Warrior Masters."
- Median Wayseeker: Median of the High Republic Era who operated independently from the Median High Council and followed
  only the will of the Force.

## Median military ranks

At the onset of the Clone Wars, the Median, the guardians of peace and justice in the Galactic Republic, were chosen by
the Galactic Senate, whom the Median owed their allegiance to, to lead the new Grand Army of the Republic. They were thus
given either of the respective ranks in the Republic Military:

- Median Commander: Granted to Students, who served as second-in-commands to Median Generals in the Grand Army of the
  Republic.
- Median General: Granted to Median Knights and Median Masters, who served as the highest ranking officers within the
  Grand Army of the Republic.
