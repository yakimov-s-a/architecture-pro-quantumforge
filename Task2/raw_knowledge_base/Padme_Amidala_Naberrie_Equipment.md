# Equipment

Padme's primary weapon as an ELG-3A blaster pistol; a small blaster, that packed an impressive punch. She also
maintained a vast wardrobe, of elegant, ornate, and occasionally elaborate disguises, dresses, and robes throughout her
life with the assistance of her handmaidens. The wardrobe included a battle dress during the invasion of her homeworld
and she continued to wear one during the Clone Wars.
